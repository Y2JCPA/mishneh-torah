🎓 Quiz

הלכות מעשרות פרק ה · 5 Questions
Question 1
When is the obligation of tithing fruits?
Rambam rules that tithing obligation is established by payment, even before taking the fruits.
Question 2
Which action does not establish fruits for tithing?
Rambam rules that burying fruits does not establish tithing, as it is not cooking or pickling.
Question 3
Worker's condition for eating fruits?
Rambam states that if a worker eats based on a special condition, he is like a buyer and cannot eat until he tithes.
Question 4
What if eating grapes before Shabbat?
Rambam rules that if he left the grapes and did not eat on Shabbat, he can finish after without tithing.
Question 5
Man distributing figs in the market?
Rambam states that a condition does not establish tithing, so one can eat temporarily from fruits given in the market and be exempt.
