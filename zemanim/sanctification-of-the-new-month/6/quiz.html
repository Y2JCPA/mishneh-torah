הלכות קידוש החודש פרק ו · 5 Questions
Question 1
What is the duration of the interval between one conjunction of the moon and the sun?
Halachah 3 states that the interval is 29 full days, 12 hours of the thirtieth day, and 793 units.
Question 2
How many leap years are included in a nineteen-year cycle?
Halachah 10 mentions that there are seven leap years in a nineteen-year cycle.
Question 3
What is the remainder for an ordinary lunar year when counted in groups of seven?
Halachah 5 specifies the remainder for an ordinary lunar year as 4 days, 8 hours, and 876 units.
Question 4
What is the starting point for calculating the conjunction of the first nineteen-year cycle?
Halachah 13 states that the conjunction marking the beginning of the first nineteen-year cycle was on 2 - 5 - 204.
Question 5
When you add the remainder of a lunar month to the conjunction time, what day is determined for the next month?
Halachah 6 indicates that adding 1 - 12 - 793 will help determine the conjunction for the following month.
