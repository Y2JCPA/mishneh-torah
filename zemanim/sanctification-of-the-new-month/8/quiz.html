הלכות קידוש החודש פרק ח · 5 Questions
Question 1
What is the maximum duration of a lunar month?
A lunar month is defined to be either 29 or 30 days long as stated in Halachah 2.
Question 2
Which month is always considered full in the fixed calendar?
Halachah 5 establishes Tishrei as always being a full month.
Question 3
How many days between Rosh HaShanah indicates a year of lacking months?
Halachah 7 specifies that two days between Rosh HaShanah celebrations results in a year of lacking months.
Question 4
What happens if Rosh HaShanah falls on a Tuesday?
Halachah 10 explains that if Rosh HaShanah falls on a Tuesday, the months will proceed in order.
Question 5
In a leap year, what indicates that the months will be complete?
Halachah 8 states that six days between Rosh HaShanah in a leap year leads to complete months.
