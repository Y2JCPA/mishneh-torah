הלכות קידוש החודש פרק ג · 5 Questions
Question 1
What is the maximum distance that witnesses can be from the court to testify about the new moon?
Witnesses can travel a distance of a night and a day to testify, as stated in Halacha 1.
Question 2
Which two months allow Sabbath violations for the sake of witness testimony?
Sabbath violations are permitted for witness testimony specifically for Rosh Chodesh Nisan and Tishrei, as detailed in Halacha 2.
Question 3
How long did the High Court originally accept testimony regarding the new moon?
Originally, the High Court accepted testimony throughout the entire thirtieth day, as noted in Halacha 5.
Question 4
What happens if witnesses arrive after the time of minchah?
If witnesses arrive after the time of minchah, the following day should be observed as a holiday, as stated in Halacha 6.
Question 5
What was instituted as a response to the Samaritans lighting bonfires?
The Sages instituted the practice of sending messengers instead of lighting bonfires due to the confusion caused by the Samaritans, as mentioned in Halacha 8.
