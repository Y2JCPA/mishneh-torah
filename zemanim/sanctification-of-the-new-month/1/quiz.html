הלכות קידוש החודש פרק א · 5 Questions
Question 1
What determines the beginning of the new month?
The new month begins with the sighting of the moon as described in Halacha 3.
Question 2
How many days does the moon remain hidden each month?
The moon is hidden for approximately two days each month according to Halacha 3.
Question 3
What is a month called if it has 29 days?
A month with 29 days is referred to as chaseir, meaning 'lacking' as per Halacha 4.
Question 4
Who has the authority to declare Rosh Chodesh?
The establishment of Rosh Chodesh is reserved for the High Court, as stated in Halacha 5.
Question 5
Where must the calculations for the months occur?
Calculations and establishment of months occur only in Eretz Yisrael as stated in Halacha 8.
