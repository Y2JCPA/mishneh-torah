הלכות קידוש החודש פרק ט · 5 Questions
Question 1
What is the opinion of some Sages regarding the length of a solar year?
Some Sages maintain that a solar year is 365 days and 1/4 of a day (6 hours) according to Halacha 1.
Question 2
How many days and hours are added for each complete year within a nineteen-year cycle?
According to Halacha 4, ten days, twenty-one hours, and 204 units are added for each complete year.
Question 3
What must be done after determining the number of complete years to find the vernal equinox?
After determining the number of complete years, 7 days must be added according to Halacha 6.
Question 4
How much time has the vernal equinox advanced over six millennia according to Halacha 4?
The vernal equinox will have advanced approximately fifteen days over six millennia as mentioned in Halacha 4.
Question 5
What is the maximum number of days one may need to add when determining the day of the week for the equinox?
One should never need to add more than one, two, or three days, or in rare cases, four days as stated in Halacha 8.
