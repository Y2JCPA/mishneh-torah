הלכות קידוש החודש פרק ז · 5 Questions
Question 1
Which days cannot Rosh Chodesh Tishrei be established on?
According to Halachah 1, Rosh Chodesh Tishrei should never be established on Sunday, Wednesday, or Friday.
Question 2
What happens if the conjunction occurs after noon on a Monday?
As stated in Halachah 2, if the conjunction takes place at noon or after, Rosh Chodesh is established on the following day.
Question 3
What is the rationale for postponing Rosh Chodesh if the conjunction occurs at noon or later?
Halachah 2 explains that the moon is not visible until hours after the conjunction, necessitating a postponement.
Question 4
In an ordinary year, if the conjunction occurs late on the third day, what day is Rosh Chodesh established?
Halachah 4 states that if the conjunction occurs after the specified time, Rosh Chodesh is established on Thursday.
Question 5
If the conjunction occurs on Monday after three hours and 589 units, when is Rosh Chodesh observed?
According to Halachah 5, Rosh Chodesh is established on Tuesday in this situation.
