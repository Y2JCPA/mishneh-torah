הלכות קידוש החודש פרק ה · 5 Questions
Question 1
Who has the authority to sanctify Rosh Chodesh according to Halacha 1?
Halacha 1 states that only the Sanhedrin or a semichah court in Eretz Yisrael can sanctify Rosh Chodesh.
Question 2
What is the current practice regarding Rosh HaShanah celebration as outlined in Halacha 8?
Halacha 8 indicates that Rosh HaShanah is celebrated for two days in the diaspora due to historical practices.
Question 3
According to Halacha 5, why do the inhabitants of Eretz Yisrael celebrate one day of holidays?
Halacha 5 explains that the fixed calendar allows for the determination of holidays, leading to one-day celebrations.
Question 4
In Halacha 12, what should inhabitants do if there is no established custom?
Halacha 12 states that if there is no established custom, they should celebrate two days as is customary in the world.
Question 5
What is mentioned in Halacha 11 about places more than a ten-day journey from Jerusalem?
Halacha 11 indicates that communities beyond a ten-day journey should continue the custom of celebrating two days.
