הלכות קידוש החודש פרק ב · 5 Questions
Question 1
Who is allowed to testify about the sighting of the new moon?
Only the testimony of two adult males is acceptable as per Halacha 1.
Question 2
What must the court do if they do not know the character of the witnesses?
Halacha 3 states that the court must send character witnesses to verify the integrity of the moon sighting witnesses.
Question 3
If a witness saw the moon in a reflection, what is the ruling?
Halacha 5 specifies that only direct sightings of the moon are acceptable for sanctification.
Question 4
How many judges are necessary to declare the new month sanctified?
Halacha 8 states that a minimum of three judges is required to sanctify the new moon.
Question 5
What happens if the court sanctifies the new month based on mistaken testimony?
Halacha 10 indicates that once sanctified, the new month remains valid regardless of errors in testimony.
