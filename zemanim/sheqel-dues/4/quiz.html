🎓 Quiz

הלכות שקלים פרק ד · 5 Questions
Question 1
What are the funds from terumat halishcah primarily used for?
As stated in Halachah 1, terumat halishcah funds are used for communal sacrifices and their accompanying needs.
Question 2
What should be done if there are insufficient funds from the shekalim for communal offerings?
Halachah 10 specifies that if the collection from the shekalim is insufficient, funds should be taken from Bedek HaBayit.
Question 3
When should the communal offerings begin to be brought from the new collection of shekalim?
Halachah 11 states that from Rosh Chodesh Nisan onward, the communal offerings should be brought from the new collection of shekalim.
Question 4
What happens to the remainder of the incense offering after Rosh Chodesh Nisan?
Halachah 12 indicates that the remainder of the incense offering is transferred to artisans as their wages.
Question 5
Who receives wages from terumat halishcah according to Halachah 7?
Halachah 7 specifies that both scribes who check Torah scrolls and judges presiding over robbery cases receive their wages from terumat halishcah.
