הלכות שקלים פרק ב · 5 Questions
Question 1
What was the purpose of the two chests used by moneychangers?
They positioned two chests to separate the shekalim of the present year from those of the previous year (H1).
Question 2
How many chests were there in the Temple for shekalim?
The Temple had thirteen chests for various offerings and purposes (H2).
Question 3
What must be done with funds remaining after purchasing sacrifices?
The court stipulated that all remaining funds must be used to offer animals as burnt offerings (H3).
Question 4
On which occasions were funds taken from the chamber?
Funds were taken on Rosh Chodesh Nisan, Rosh Chodesh Tishrei, and fifteen days before Shavuot (H5).
Question 5
What should the person setting aside funds avoid wearing?
The person should not enter wearing a garment in which he could hide money to avoid suspicion (H10).
