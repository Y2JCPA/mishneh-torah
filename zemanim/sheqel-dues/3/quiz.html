🎓 Quiz

הלכות שקלים פרק ג · 5 Questions
Question 1
What is required if two individuals who are obligated to give shekalim contribute a shekel together?
According to Halachah 1, they are obligated to give a kolbon when giving a shekel together.
Question 2
If someone gives a shekel on behalf of a poor person as a gift, what is the kolbon obligation?
As per Halachah 3, no kolbon is required when the half-shekel is given as a gift.
Question 3
What happens if a person's shekel is lost before reaching the Temple treasury?
Halachah 8 states that if the shekel is lost, the individual must pay their half-shekel a second time.
Question 4
What is the consequence if an agent misappropriates a half-shekel that was meant for the Temple treasury?
Halachah 10 indicates that if the agent intentionally misappropriates the funds, they receive lashes.
Question 5
If someone gives a half-shekel from consecrated funds unknowingly, what is the status of that half-shekel?
Halachah 11 explains that funds given unknowingly from consecrated sources are not considered consecrated.
