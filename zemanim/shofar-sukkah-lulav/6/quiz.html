הלכות שופר וסוכה ולולב פרק ו · 5 Questions
Question 1
A tumtum (person of undetermined sex) — what is their sukkah obligation?
Halacha 1: a tumtum and androginos are obligated due to doubt — because their status is uncertain, they must fulfill the mitzvah.
Question 2
On the first night of Sukkot it is raining — what is the law regarding eating in the sukkah?
Halacha 7: eating in the sukkah on the first night is obligatory. One must eat at least a k'zayit even if it rains, unlike the remaining nights.
Question 3
A person sits with head and body inside the sukkah but the table is outside — what is the ruling?
Halacha 8: this is forbidden because the table pulls him toward the outside. It is considered as though he did not eat inside the sukkah at all.
Question 4
On the seventh day of Sukkot, when may one begin dismantling the sukkah?
Halacha 11: one may not dismantle after the seventh-day morning meal, but from minchah onward may bring utensils inside for Shmini Atzeret.
Question 5
Foods hung as sukkah decorations fell during the festival — may one eat them?
Halacha 16: foods and drinks hung as decorations are forbidden for all eight days — even if they fall, they remain forbidden.
