🚫 Absolute Prohibition
Eating chametz on Pesach: karet (spiritual excision). Even an olive's worth. The prohibition begins at midday on the 14th of Nisan (erev Pesach). From midday: Torah prohibition. The Sages added two hours before — from the 5th hour, chametz is rabbinically forbidden. Chametz must be completely eliminated: eating, benefit, and even owning it are all forbidden. No other food prohibition is this total.
