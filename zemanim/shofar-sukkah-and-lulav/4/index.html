הלכה י״ז׳
🔥 Height Regulations
The inner height of a sukkah must not exceed twenty cubits, and structures within it must also comply with halachic requirements to maintain its validity.

🎓 Key Principles

Chapter 4
🔥
Minimum Measurements
A sukkah must have specific minimum dimensions to be considered valid.
🔥
Wall Requirements
A sukkah requires three complete walls to be kosher.
🔥
Height Limitations
A sukkah's height must not exceed twenty cubits to remain valid.
🔥
Structural Integrity
Walls can be constructed from various materials, including natural elements, as long as they fulfill halachic criteria.
