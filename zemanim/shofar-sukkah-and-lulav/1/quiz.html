🎓 Quiz

הלכות שופר וסוכה ולולב פרק א · 5 Questions
Question 1
What is the primary focus of the mitzvah regarding the shofar?
The mitzvah is to listen to the shofar being blown, as stated in Halacha 1.
Question 2
Which type of shofar is unacceptable for use?
A shofar from an apostate city is not valid, as mentioned in Halacha 3.
Question 3
What is the minimum size requirement for a shofar?
The minimum size of a shofar is defined as sufficient to hold with ends visibly protruding, which is a handbreadth, as per Halacha 5.
Question 4
What must be avoided when obtaining a shofar during the festival?
It is forbidden to violate any festival laws, including sh'vut, to obtain a shofar, as stated in Halacha 4.
Question 5
If someone hears the sound of a shofar inside a cave, what condition must be met for them to fulfill their obligation?
To fulfill their obligation, they must hear the shofar's sound alone without echoes, as explained in Halacha 8.
