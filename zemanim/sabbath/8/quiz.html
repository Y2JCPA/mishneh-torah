🎓 Quiz

הלכות שבת פרק ח · 5 Questions
Question 1
A person waters a houseplant on Shabbat. Which melachah category does this violate?
Watering is a toldah of sowing because it promotes plant growth — the same creative purpose as planting a seed.
Question 2
Squeezing juice from fruit on Shabbat violates which melachah?
Threshing separates food from its natural covering. Squeezing juice from fruit does the same — extracting the liquid from its "container."
Question 3
What is the minimum amount of plowing that creates liability?
Even the tiniest amount of ground preparation — digging the smallest hole — constitutes plowing on Shabbat.
Question 4
Picking a fruit from a tree on Shabbat violates which melachah?
Reaping = detaching anything that grows from the ground. Picking fruit, pulling up vegetables, plucking flowers — all reaping.
Question 5
Mixing flour with water on Shabbat violates which melachah?
Kneading: combining dry materials with liquid to form a cohesive mass. Mixing flour and water is the classic example.
