לרפואת פייגא בת יטא רבקה

🎓 Quiz

הלכות כלים פרק ט״ז · 5 Questions
Question 1
What is the minimum remaining size of a large oven that keeps it susceptible?
Halacha 1: the remnants of a larger oven that keep it susceptible are four handbreadths.
Question 2
How does an owner purify an impure oven that he does not want to destroy?
Halacha 1: to restore purity to an oven the owner does not want to destroy, divide it into three portions.
Question 3
An oven cut into rings with sand between rings and clay-coated from outside — is it susceptible?
Halacha 4: when an oven was cut into rings, sand placed between each ring, and the entire structure coated with clay from outside, it is susceptible.
Question 4
When one portion of an oven divided into three is as large as the other two combined — what is the status of the large portion?
Halacha 2: when an impure oven is divided into three portions and one is as large as the other two, the large portion is impure.
Question 5
An oven made from earthenware shards assembled with clay coating on both inside and outside — is it susceptible?
Halacha 6: when earthenware shards are formed into an oven with coating on inside and outside, it is susceptible.
