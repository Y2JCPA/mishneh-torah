הלכות כלים פרק ח · 5 Questions
Question 1
An unfinished metal vessel still needing to be smoothed — is it susceptible to impurity?
Halacha 1: metal keilim are not susceptible until totally completed. Halacha 4 notes this is the reverse of wooden vessels.
Question 2
Are ornaments made for animals — like rings for an animal's neck — susceptible to impurity?
Halachot 5-6 distinguish human ornaments (susceptible) from ornaments for animals and vessels (pure).
Question 3
Can a vessel become susceptible to impurity through thought alone?
Halacha 9: all keilim can become susceptible through thought. However, they do not lose susceptibility unless a deed is performed.
Question 4
A bell found anywhere outside a large city — is it presumed susceptible or pure?
Halacha 13: wherever bells are found they are susceptible to impurity, except in large cities where the majority are made for doors.
Question 5
What is the status of all masks (facial coverings)?
Halacha 8 explicitly states that all masks are susceptible to impurity.
