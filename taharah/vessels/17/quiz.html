הלכות כלים פרק י״ז · 5 Questions
Question 1
The 'crown' (rim) of a range — is it susceptible to impurity?
Halacha 3: the crown of a range is pure.
Question 2
An extra earth layer on top of a baker's oven — is it susceptible?
Halacha 2: the extra layer of earth on a baker's oven is susceptible like the oven itself — unlike a homeowner's where it is pure.
Question 3
A trivet three fingerbreadths or less in height placed on an impure range — what is the trivet's status?
Halacha 6: trivets that are three fingerbreadths high or less follow the range's status.
Question 4
A range made for baking — does it follow oven measures or range measures?
Halacha 1: a range made for baking follows oven measures; if made for cooking, range measures.
Question 5
When is the open space in front of a range considered joined to the range?
Halacha 5: the open space in front of a range is considered joined to the range when elevated three fingerbreadths above the ground.
