הלכות מטמאי משכב ומושב פרק ה · 5 Questions
Question 1
If a woman after childbirth does not immerse after the impure days, what is the status of any subsequent blood she sees?
Halacha 1 states that a woman after childbirth who has not immersed after her impure days remains a nidah, and any blood she sees thereafter is nidah blood.
Question 2
If a zavah discharges blood while descending to immerse, what must she do?
Halacha 2 rules that if the blood emerged from inside the uterus, she has not yet concluded her zivah and must count seven clean days again.
Question 3
A major zavah immerses on her seventh spotless day. What restriction applies until evening?
Halacha 8 states: 'A zav and a major zavah who immerse on their seventh spotless day should not touch articles that are ritually pure until the evening, lest they experience an impure discharge.'
Question 4
If a zavah immerses on the morning of the twelfth day and then is intimate, what is the man's status?
Halacha 7 rules: 'Although she is forbidden to engage in intimacy until the evening, if she was intimate, the man with whom she was intimate is pure.'
Question 5
Does a minor zavah who immerses on the day she is watching achieve certain purity?
Halacha 5 states: 'When a minor zavah immerses on the day she is watching after sunrise, the status of the articles she touches and a man with whom she is intimate is indefinite.'
