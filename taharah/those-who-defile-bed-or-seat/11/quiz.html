הלכות מטמאי משכב ומושב פרק י״א · 5 Questions
Question 1
Why is the word of unlearned persons accepted for all stages of red heifer purification?
Halacha 1 states: 'Because of the severity involved, the matter is never treated lightly' — making all persons trustworthy in this unique context.
Question 2
Within what geographic area is the word of unlearned persons accepted for earthenware vessels?
Halacha 5 states: 'From Modi'it and closer, the word of unlearned people is accepted with regard to earthenware containers. Outside that area, their word is not accepted.'
Question 3
What happens to opened barrels or dough left after a pilgrimage festival?
Halacha 9 rules: 'Once the festival passes, the remainder of the contents of the barrel and the remainder of the dough are assumed to be impure.'
Question 4
On the night after a pilgrimage festival, what did the priests do with Temple utensils?
Halacha 10 states: 'After the festival, on the night following the festival, the priests would immerse all the utensils that were in the Temple in a mikveh, because the unlearned people had touched them during the festival.'
Question 5
Are pots taken directly from a kiln presumed pure?
Halacha 7 states: 'When one takes earthenware containers from a kiln no matter where it is located, they are considered pure, both with regard to sacrificial foods and with regard to terumah.'
