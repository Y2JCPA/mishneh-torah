הלכות פרה אדומה פרק ט · 5 Questions
Question 1
How must the ashes be added to the water during sanctification?
Halacha 2 rules that the sanctifier must cast the ashes onto the water by hand with focused concentration.
Question 2
If a small container is inside a large container and you sanctify the large container's water, is the small container's water also sanctified?
Halacha 4 rules that a small container inside a large container maintains its own separate status — sanctifying the outer water does not sanctify the inner container's water.
Question 3
What happens when any amount of non-sanctified water is mixed into sanctified water?
Halacha 8 states that even the smallest amount of non-sanctified water mixed into sanctified water disqualifies the entire contents.
Question 4
If a domesticated animal drinks from sanctified water, what is the ruling?
Halacha 12 states that when a domesticated or wild animal drinks from sanctified water, the water is disqualified.
Question 5
If a person merely thinks about drinking the sanctified water without acting on that thought, does the water become disqualified?
Halacha 15 explicitly rules that thinking about drinking sanctified water does not disqualify it — an actual action is required.
