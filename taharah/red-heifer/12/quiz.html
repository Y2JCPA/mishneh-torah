הלכות פרה אדומה פרק י״ב · 5 Questions
Question 1
When sprinkling purifies a corpse-impure person, are his clothes also purified?
Halacha 1 rules that when water is sprinkled on a person impure from a corpse, both he and his clothing are purified at the same time.
Question 2
A needle on a shard was sprinkled with purification water but there is doubt whether the water reached the needle. What is the ruling?
Halacha 3 rules that when there is doubt whether sprinkling reached the needle, we rule strictly that it has not been purified.
Question 3
What minimum number of wool blankets joined together are treated as a single entity for sprinkling purposes?
Halacha 7 states that three wool blankets joined together (or six of linen) meet the threshold to be treated as a single entity for sprinkling.
Question 4
A spindle has three parts. How must sprinkling be applied to purify it?
Halacha 10 states that while the spindle parts are joined for impurity, they are separated for sprinkling — each part must be individually and specifically sprinkled.
Question 5
Does sprinkling the exterior of a vessel purify the interior of its hollow handle?
Halacha 12 rules that all hollow handles of vessels — like a knife handle — are considered part of the vessel, so sprinkling the exterior purifies the handle's interior as well.
