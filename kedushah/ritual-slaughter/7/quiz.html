🎓 Quiz

הלכות שחיטה פרק ז · 5 Questions
Question 1
If only the lower of the lung's two membranes is perforated, what is the ruling?
Perforation of only one membrane is permitted because the other continues to protect the lung.
Question 2
According to the Rambam's great encompassing principle, what does it mean if a lung placed in lukewarm water produces no bubbles when blown?
No bubbling in lukewarm water means the lung is whole and unpunctured — a definitive leniency.
Question 3
Two boils are found adjacent to each other on a lung. What is the ruling?
The proximity of two boils makes a perforation between them very likely, and there is no viable method of inspection.
Question 4
A needle is found inside a lung that was cut open before being inflated. What is the ruling?
If the lung was cut before inflation, the air-test is impossible and we rule forbidden.
Question 5
Which of the following is NOT one of the five forbidden colors of the lung?
Blackish-blue is a permitted color, not a forbidden one.
