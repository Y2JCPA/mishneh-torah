הלכות מאכלות אסורות פרק ג · 5 Questions
Question 1
Why is bee honey permitted even though bees are non-kosher?
Honey is permitted because bees collect nectar from herbs and store it — it is not produced by the bee's body.
Question 2
Why did the Sages decree against gentile cheese?
The forbidden curdling agent causes the entire cheese to form, rendering all of it forbidden.
Question 3
Blood spot found on the yolk of a fertilized egg — ruling?
When blood is on the yolk, the entire egg is forbidden. On the white, only the blood is discarded.
Question 4
Which egg shape definitely indicates a non-kosher species?
Both ends rounded, both pointed, or yolk on outside = definitely non-kosher.
Question 5
What renders an open barrel of gentile brine permissible?
Even one kosher fish with identifiable head and backbone permits the entire barrel.
