הלכות מאכלות אסורות פרק א · 5 Questions
Question 1
How many signs must a land animal have to be kosher?
Both signs are required together. The Torah highlights the camel (cud only) and pig (hooves only) to teach this.
Question 2
A non-kosher animal gives birth to an offspring that looks exactly like a sheep with all kosher signs. Halacha?
Offspring follow the mother's species regardless of appearance.
Question 3
Key legal difference between a kosher wild beast and a kosher domesticated animal?
Wild beast's fat is permitted and its blood must be covered; domesticated animal's fat is forbidden.
Question 4
Most reliable way to determine a bird species is kosher?
A reliable community tradition is the preferred basis. The four signs are used only when no tradition exists.
Question 5
Which sign is the practical test for kosher fish?
Scales are the decisive sign. Even if they fall off after leaving the water, the fish is kosher.
