🎓 Quiz

הלכות מאכלות אסורות פרק ה · 5 Questions
Question 1
Minimum amount of a limb from a living animal to incur lashes?
Lashes apply only for eating an olive-sized (kazayit) portion.
Question 2
Which boneless organ is subject to the ever min hachai prohibition?
The tongue, testicles, spleen, kidneys, and heart are listed as qualifying boneless organs.
Question 3
If tearing a limb makes the animal trefe simultaneously, how many prohibitions are violated?
Both prohibitions take effect simultaneously, so neither pre-empts the other — double liability applies.
Question 4
A fetus protrudes its leg, it's returned inside, and the mother is slaughtered. Status of that leg?
Once a fetus's limb protrudes, it is forbidden forever — even if returned and even after slaughter.
Question 5
A full-term living fetus is found inside a properly slaughtered kosher animal. What is required?
The fetus is permitted through the mother's slaughter alone. Only if it steps on the ground does it need its own shechitah.
