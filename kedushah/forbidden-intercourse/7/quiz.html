הלכות איסורי ביאה פרק ז · 5 Questions
Question 1
What is the essential condition for bleeding to qualify as blood of the throes?
Continuous suffering until delivery is the defining criterion for dam rodim.
Question 2
If labor pains cease for three full days during the zivah period while bleeding continues, how is it classified?
Once pains fully stop for three or more days during zivah, the bleeding becomes full zivah bleeding.
Question 3
How many consecutive clean days must a yoledet b'zivah count before immersing?
She must count seven consecutive days free of uterine bleeding before immersing.
Question 4
For a woman with a fixed pattern of one impure day, one pure day, when may she have relations?
Each impure day triggers niddah. She must count seven clean days, making the eighth day the first permitted day.
Question 5
What happens if a yoledet b'zivah never stops bleeding after birth?
Without seven spotless days, the blood-of-purity exemption is inapplicable. All bleeding remains zivah.
