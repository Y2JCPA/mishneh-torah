🎓 Quiz

הלכות איסורי ביאה פרק ח · 5 Questions
Question 1
What two conditions must both be present for a valid veset?
Both a fixed recurring time and consistent physical symptoms are required. Neither alone suffices.
Question 2
A woman whose veset was the 15th menstruates on the 16th. Which days are forbidden next month?
Both days are forbidden: the 15th due to the original veset, the 16th because of the deviation.
Question 3
Can a veset be established if menstruation repeatedly occurs after jumping?
A veset caused by an external factor is never valid, regardless of recurrence.
Question 4
Minimum clean days a woman who erred in counting must wait after bleeding stops?
She must count a minimum of 7 clean days, maximum 17.
Question 5
Bleeding from a uterine wound at the exact time of her veset — her status?
Wound blood does not render her impure, even at the time of her veset.
