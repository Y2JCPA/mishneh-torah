🎓 Quiz

הלכות איסורי ביאה פרק ה · 5 Questions
Question 1
From where must blood originate to render a woman impure as a niddah?
Only blood from the uterus — the Source — renders a woman impure.
Question 2
Which is NOT among the five impure colors?
The five impure colors are red, black, bright saffron, muddy water, and diluted wine. Yellow is not listed.
Question 3
A stain is lighter than the standard shade. Ruling?
If the stain is lighter than the standard shade, the woman is pure.
Question 4
What is the status of hymeneal bleeding?
Hymeneal bleeding is pure because it originates from a wound, not the uterus.
Question 5
A woman excretes blood together with urine. Ruling?
Blood excreted with urine is assumed to come from the urinary tract, not the uterus.
