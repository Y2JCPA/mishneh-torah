🎓 Quiz

הלכות איסורי ביאה פרק ג · 5 Questions
Question 1
What is the punishment for a man who has relations with a consecrated maiden?
Both the man and the consecrated maiden are executed by stoning, but only when she is still a virgin, consecrated, and in her father's home.
Question 2
When the daughter of a priest commits adultery while married, how is she executed?
The Torah mandates burning for the married daughter of a priest who commits adultery.
Question 3
If ten men have relations with a consecrated maiden, what happens to the first?
The first man is stoned because she was still a virgin. The remaining nine receive strangulation.
Question 4
What defines a shifchah charufah?
She is owned jointly — one partner released her, one did not — and she has been consecrated to a Hebrew servant.
Question 5
What penalty does the shifchah charufah receive?
She receives lashes, while the man brings a guilt offering — unique among all forbidden relations.
