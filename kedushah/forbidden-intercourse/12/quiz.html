🎓 Quiz

הלכות איסורי ביאה פרק י״ב · 5 Questions
Question 1
At what level of law is marriage between a Jew and a gentile prohibited?
Based on Deut. 7:3, it is a full Scriptural prohibition punishable by lashes.
Question 2
A zealot who kills a man publicly cohabiting with a gentile woman is considered:
When the act occurs publicly before ten or more Jews, the zealot is praised, modeled on Pinchas.
Question 3
What makes relations with a gentile uniquely harmful compared to other forbidden unions?
Unlike other forbidden unions where the child remains Jewish, a child of a gentile woman follows her status and is lost entirely.
Question 4
Which male converts are forever forbidden from marrying native-born Jews?
Male Ammonite and Moabite converts are forbidden forever. Female converts may marry immediately.
Question 5
Why did David permanently ban the Netinim (Gibeonites)?
David extended the ban permanently after the Gibeonites lacked mercy, demanding seven of Saul's sons be hanged.
