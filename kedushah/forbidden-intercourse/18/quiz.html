🎓 Quiz

הלכות איסורי ביאה פרק י״ח · 5 Questions
Question 1
Which renders a woman a halachic zonah?
Relations with a gentile render a woman a zonah. Relations with an unmarried Jewish man do not trigger this status.
Question 2
Status of a kohen's wife who was raped?
The wife of a kohen who is raped becomes forbidden to her husband. He receives lashes if he continues relations.
Question 3
On what principle is a captive woman's own claim of purity accepted?
Since we only suspect her based on her own disclosure of captivity, her claim of purity in that same statement is accepted.
Question 4
A city was besieged with no escape. What is presumed about the women?
When all exits were blocked, all women are presumed defiled and forbidden to the priesthood.
Question 5
If one hiding place existed during a siege, how does this affect all the women?
Even one escape route grants every woman a miggo — one possibility saves the entire community.
