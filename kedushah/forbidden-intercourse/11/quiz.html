🎓 Quiz

הלכות איסורי ביאה פרק י״א · 5 Questions
Question 1
What universal stringency did Jewish women accept regarding uterine bleeding?
Any bleeding — even a mustard-seed-sized drop — requires counting seven spotless days before immersing.
Question 2
What must a bride do before marital relations are permitted?
Any woman who consents to marry must count seven spotless days afterward and immerse.
Question 3
Can immersing in a bath substitute for mikveh immersion?
Even if all the water in the world passes over her in a bath, she remains impure. Only a valid mikveh restores purity.
Question 4
Which practice did the Rambam condemn as heretical?
Abstaining for 40/80 days after birth regardless of bleeding is heretical error derived from the Sadducees.
Question 5
During the seven spotless days, which is explicitly permitted?
A woman may adorn herself during niddah and spotless days so she does not become unattractive, while all physical contact remains forbidden.
