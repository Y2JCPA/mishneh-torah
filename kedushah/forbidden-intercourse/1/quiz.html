🎓 Quiz

הלכות איסורי ביאה פרק א · 5 Questions
Question 1
What minimum physical act creates full liability for forbidden intercourse?
Inserting the corona constitutes full liability — complete intercourse is not required.
Question 2
Which form of execution applies to adultery?
Adultery is punishable by strangulation. Any Torah death penalty stated without specifying a method defaults to strangulation.
Question 3
If a person was compelled to engage in forbidden relations, what is his liability?
A compelled person bears no liability at all — no lashes, no sacrifice — based on Deuteronomy 22:26.
Question 4
Two adult males engage in homosexual intercourse. What punishment?
Both parties are liable to stoning, derived from Leviticus 18:22.
Question 5
A couple arrives from overseas and lives publicly as husband and wife. How does the court treat them?
If they establish the public presumption of a married couple, they are treated as married.
