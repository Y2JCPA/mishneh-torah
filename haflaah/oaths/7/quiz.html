הלכות שבועות פרק ז · 5 Questions
Question 1
Can sh'vuat hapikadon be triggered by a claim on land?
Sh'vuat hapikadon covers movable property only. Land, servants, and promissory notes are explicitly excluded.
Question 2
He falsely denies the same deposit four separate times in four separate oaths. His liability:
Multiple oaths on the same denied claim create only a single liability — repetition does not compound.
Question 3
Five different people each claim a deposit from him and he denies each. His liability:
Each separate claimant with a separate claim creates independent liability — five claims = five obligations.
Question 4
He denies a claim worth half a p'rutah. Is he liable for sh'vuat hapikadon?
A p'rutah is the minimum threshold of financial significance. Denials below this amount are not actionable.
Question 5
The oath must be administered in:
For liability, the oath must be in a language the defendant comprehends — administration in an unknown language does not bind.
