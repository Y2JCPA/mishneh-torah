הלכות נזירות פרק ה · 5 Questions
Question 1
A nazirite eats fresh grapes, raisins, and grape seeds together. How is the minimum measure calculated?
All forbidden grape products — fresh grapes, raisins, seeds, peels — combine together to reach the olive-size minimum for lashes (Halachah 3).
Question 2
Wine is mixed with honey and the flavor of wine cannot be detected. May a nazirite drink it?
When the wine flavor is undetectable, the mixture is permitted — the nazirite prohibition is no stricter than that of other forbidden substances (Halachah 6).
Question 3
A nazirite cuts one hair from its root using scissors. Is he liable for lashes?
A nazirite is liable for lashes for removing even one hair from its root, whether by razor, scissors, or by hand (Halachah 11).
Question 4
A nazirite unknowingly enters a tent with a corpse. He is warned but doesn't leave immediately. Is he liable?
Once warned, remaining in the tent of the dead constitutes a punishable violation — the deed of remaining makes him liable (Halachah 19).
Question 5
A nazirite voluntarily makes himself impure from a corpse. How many prohibition does he violate?
By voluntarily defiling himself, he violates both the specific nazirite prohibition and the prohibition of 'do not delay in paying' his nazirite vow (Halachah 21).
