הלכות סוטה פרק א · 5 Questions
Question 1
How long must a wife remain in seclusion with the warned man to become forbidden to her husband?
Halacha 2 states she must remain 'long enough to roast an egg and swallow it' for the prohibition to take effect.
Question 2
What happens if the husband forgoes his warning after his wife has already entered into seclusion with the warned man?
Halacha 7 rules that the warning cannot be nullified after she enters into privacy with him.
Question 3
When a husband is overseas and the court issues a warning to his wife on his behalf, what is the court's primary purpose?
Halacha 10 explains that the court's warning is to disqualify her from receiving her ketubah.
Question 4
If a woman already drank the bitter water because of a specific man and survived, and her husband warns her about the same man again, what happens if she is then secluded with him?
Halacha 12 states that she cannot drink a second time for the same man; instead she is forbidden forever and divorced without her ketubah.
Question 5
If one acceptable witness testifies that a sotah committed adultery, and a second acceptable witness immediately contradicts him, what is the ruling?
Halacha 17 rules that when both testify simultaneously, she must drink the bitter water.
