🎓 Quiz

הלכות סוטה פרק ד · 5 Questions
Question 1
On which date does the court specially review which women should be compelled to drink the bitter water?
Halacha 1 states that on the fifteenth of Adar the court attends to communal needs, including determining which women should be compelled to drink.
Question 2
If a sotah says 'I will not drink' while calm and unafraid, what happens?
Halacha 3 rules that a calm, deliberate refusal cannot be retracted.
Question 3
If a sotah refuses to drink before the scroll has been blotted out, what happens to the scroll?
Halacha 4 rules that the scroll is entombed and is not fit for another sotah.
Question 4
Which of the following disqualifies a sotah scroll?
Halacha 9 rules that using kankantum or any permanently marking substance renders the scroll unacceptable.
Question 5
Through gilgul sh'vuah, which period may a husband NOT include in his wife's sotah oath?
Halacha 17 explains the oath cannot include periods when infidelity would not make her forbidden to him.
