לרפואת פייגא בת יטא רבקה

🎓 Quiz

הלכות אישות פרק י״ד · 5 Questions
Question 1
According to Halacha 1, how often must a worker (פועל) fulfill his conjugal duties toward his wife?
Halacha 1 specifies that a worker (פועל) owes conjugal duties once a week.
Question 2
If a husband makes a vow forbidding marital relations with his wife, what happens after one week if he has not absolved the vow?
Halacha 6 states that after the one-week respite he must either divorce his wife and pay her ketubah or absolve the vow.
Question 3
If a wife rebels against her husband because she is repulsed by him and cannot engage in relations voluntarily, what must the court do?
Halacha 8 rules that since she is not like a captive forced to be with one she loathes, the husband is compelled to divorce her immediately.
Question 4
After the formal announcement process for a moredet who rebels to cause distress, how long passes before she forfeits her entire ketubah and is divorced?
Halacha 9 and 10 describe weekly announcements made over twelve months, after which she forfeits her ketubah entirely and is divorced.
Question 5
When a husband rebels against his wife by refusing intimacy while continuing to support her, what is the weekly financial penalty?
Halacha 15 states that a husband who rebels must increase his wife's ketubah by 36 barleycorns worth of pure silver each week.
