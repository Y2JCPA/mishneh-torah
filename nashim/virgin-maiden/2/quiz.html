הלכות נערה בתולה פרק ב · 5 Questions
Question 1
How many total payments does a rapist make, and which payment is unique to rape?
Halachah 2 states that a rapist makes four payments — the fine, embarrassment, damages, and pain — while a seducer makes only three.
Question 2
What happens to the fine if a seducer agrees to marry the girl?
Halachah 7 states that a seducer pays embarrassment and damages immediately but is not required to pay the fine unless the father refuses to allow the marriage.
Question 3
How does the court evaluate damages caused to a rape or seduction victim?
Halachah 6 teaches that damages are assessed by comparing her market value as a virgin versus a non-virgin.
Question 4
If a man admits 'I raped the daughter of so-and-so,' what is he liable for?
Halachah 12 establishes that a fine is never imposed based on self-admission, but the man's admission does obligate him to pay embarrassment and damages.
Question 5
Why does the Torah prohibit a father from arranging for his daughter to engage in relations for payment?
Halachah 17 explains that allowing fathers to hire out their daughters would lead to rampant sexual immorality.
