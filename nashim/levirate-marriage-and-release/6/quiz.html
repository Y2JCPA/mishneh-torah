🎓 Quiz

הלכות יבום וחליצה פרק ו · 5 Questions
Question 1
Which type of brother is fit to perform yibbum but NOT chalitzah?
Halacha 3 states that a deaf-mute, mentally incompetent man, and minor are fit for yibbum but not chalitzah.
Question 2
If a castrated yavam transgresses and performs yibbum, what is the legal result?
Halacha 4 rules that a castrated yavam acquires the yevamah but must divorce her with a get.
Question 3
When one wife of the deceased is forbidden to the yavam as an ervah, what is the status of the other wives?
Halacha 14 derives that all co-wives are fully exempt when one wife is an ervah.
Question 4
What must a woman do if she has two simultaneous yibbum obligations?
Halacha 27 states that a woman with two yibbum obligations must perform chalitzah but not yibbum.
Question 5
If the yavam gives a ma'amar to his yevamah and then nullifies it with a get before dying childless, what is the effect?
Halacha 29 states that once the ma'amar is nullified with a get, the double-obligation is removed.
