לרפואת פייגא בת יטא רבקה

🎓 Quiz

הלכות גירושין פרק ח · 5 Questions
Question 1
If a husband gives a conditional get and then enters privacy with his wife in the presence of two witnesses, what is the status of the divorce even if the condition is later fulfilled?
Halacha 2 states that entering privacy with one's wife in the presence of two witnesses renders the status of the divorce doubtful, even if the condition was fulfilled.
Question 2
A husband tells his wife: "Today you are not my wife, but tomorrow you will be my wife." What is the ruling?
Halacha 9 rules that this divorce is not effective, because although the couple's connection is severed on the day of the get, it is renewed afterwards.
Question 3
If a husband tells his wife: "This is your get on the condition that you never drink wine," what is the ruling?
Halacha 10 rules that the divorce is not effective when the condition binds the woman to her husband for the rest of her life.
Question 4
A husband gives his wife a get, saying: "On condition that you give me 200 zuz within 30 days." She gives the money within 30 days but he refuses to accept it. What is the ruling?
Halacha 21 rules that if the wife gives the money against the husband's will and he refuses to accept it, the divorce is unacceptable until he willingly accepts it.
Question 5
If a husband conditions the get on his wife nursing his son, without specifying any duration, and she nurses the son for one day, is the condition fulfilled?
Halacha 19 rules that when no time period is specified, fulfilling the condition for one day suffices.
