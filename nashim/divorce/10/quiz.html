🎓 Quiz

הלכות גירושין פרק י · 5 Questions
Question 1
According to Halacha 1, if a woman receives a 'void' get and remarries, what is the status of children born from her second husband?
Halacha 1 states that when a get is void by Scriptural law the woman remains fully married. Any child she bears her second husband is therefore illegitimate (a mamzer).
Question 2
According to Halacha 2, if a woman remarries after a get that is 'unacceptable' by Rabbinic decree, must she leave her second husband?
Halacha 2 explains that since the get is valid by Scriptural law, our Sages did not abrogate the second marriage.
Question 3
According to Halacha 4, to which men is a woman permanently forbidden after remarrying on the basis of a void get?
Halacha 4 rules she is forbidden to both husbands forever, even if she entered the second marriage without knowing of the prohibition.
Question 4
According to Halacha 17, when a divorced man has witnessed relations with his former wife before she marries another man, what does the law presume?
Halacha 17 establishes that a person will not enter into relations with his former wife with a licentious intent when he can treat the act as a mitzvah.
Question 5
According to Halacha 23, what is the ruling if a man's wife loses control of her mental faculties after marriage?
Halacha 23 states that if a wife loses mental or emotional faculties her husband may not divorce her until she regains stability — a Rabbinic ordinance to protect her.
