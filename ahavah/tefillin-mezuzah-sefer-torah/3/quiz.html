הלכות תפילין ומזוזה וספר תורה פרק ג · 5 Questions
Question 1
How many requirements for tefillin are classified as halachah l'Moshe miSinai?
Eight halachot l'Moshe miSinai govern tefillin construction. Each is indispensable — missing one invalidates the tefillin.
Question 2
What material must be used to sew the tefillin compartments shut?
Sinew (giddin) from a kosher animal — this specific requirement is one of the eight halachot l'Moshe miSinai.
Question 3
The knots in the tefillin straps form specific Hebrew letters. Which ones?
The arm knot = yud (י), head knot = dalet (ד), embossed letter = shin (ש). Together: Shaddai — one of God's names.
Question 4
If one of the eight requirements is missing, what is the status of the tefillin?
All eight are essential. Missing even one invalidates the tefillin completely. There's no hierarchy among them — each is equally required.
Question 5
What must the tefillin straps be made of?
Leather straps, dyed black on the outside. Both the material (leather) and color (black) are among the halachot l'Moshe miSinai.
