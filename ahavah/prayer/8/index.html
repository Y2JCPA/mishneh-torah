❌ These Cannot Be Made Up
Intentional missed prayer: no tashlumin — "the crooked cannot be straightened." Musaf missed: no makeup (it's a special additional, not a regular). Missed because of forgetfulness vs. intentional neglect matters greatly. Crossing Shabbat: cannot make up Shabbat prayers after Shabbat or vice versa. Tashlumin only works within the same day's prayer cycle.

🎓 Key Principles

Chapter 8
🔄
One Chance Policy
Miss a prayer accidentally — one makeup allowed. Miss two — only one can be recovered. The system doesn't allow indefinite accumulation.
❌
Intentional Sin Has No Easy Fix
"The crooked cannot be straightened" — deliberately skipping prayer cannot be undone by makeup. Repentance yes, tashlumin no.
📅
Day Boundaries Matter
Shabbat and weekday are separate cycles. You can't make up across that boundary.
🤔
Unintentional Gets Mercy
The whole tashlumin system exists only for accidental omissions. God distinguishes between neglect and mistakes.
