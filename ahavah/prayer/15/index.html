👥 Ten Men = Community
Ten adult Jewish men are required for: chazzan's repetition (including Kedushah), Birkat Kohanim, Torah reading (with Aliyot), Haftarah, Kaddish, and Barekhu. A minyan is the minimum definition of a communal gathering (tzibbur). Without 10 — each person prays individually. A woman cannot complete a minyan for these purposes.

🎤 Representative of the Community
The chazzan represents the entire community before God. Requirements: mature, married (preferably), with a pleasant voice, who the community finds acceptable, God-fearing, and without a questionable reputation. A young unmarried man may lead in emergencies. Most importantly: the community must accept him. A community leader who forces himself on an unwilling congregation is problematic.
