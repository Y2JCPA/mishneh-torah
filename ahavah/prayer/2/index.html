📋 Praise-Request-Thanks
The Amidah follows a fixed pattern: 3 blessings of praise (Avot, Gevurot, Kedushat Hashem), 13 requests (weekday additions for wisdom, repentance, forgiveness, etc.), 3 blessings of thanks (Avodah, Hodaah, Birkat Kohanim). On Shabbat/Yom Tov, the 13 requests are replaced with one blessing about the day.

🗣️ Quiet Voice
Say the Amidah in a quiet voice — loud enough to hear yourself, not loud enough for neighbors to hear. This follows Channah's prayer model. Stand with feet together (like angels). Bow at four specific points: start and end of first blessing, start and end of Modim. End by stepping back, bowing left, right, forward.
