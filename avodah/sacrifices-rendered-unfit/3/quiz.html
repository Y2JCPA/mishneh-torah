הלכות פסולי המוקדשין פרק ג · 5 Questions
Question 1
Holocaust on altar roof - What is its status for slaughter location?
Rambam learns from the verse - The Rambam learns from the verse that the altar itself is considered a place of slaughter, thus slaughtering on it is effective even for holy sacrifices.
Question 2
Which offerings must descend? - Which among the following must definitely descend if offered?
Animal offering not fit for fire - A burnt animal offering is not fit for fire in its current state and thus descends, unlike invalids born after the stage of validity.
Question 3
Difference between invalid offerings - What is the main difference between invalid sacrifices and invalid libations that ascended the outer altar?
Invalid offering may remain - An invalid offering may remain as it is fit for the altar fire, but wine libations do not burn on the altar and therefore descend.
Question 4
Holy vessel with dry item - What is the usual outcome of sanctification?
Measurements designated for specific type - Measurements were designated for a specific type only; a wet vessel does not sanctify dry (and vice versa), unlike a receiving vessel.
Question 5
Status of slaughtered offerings - What is the status of offerings that have been slaughtered but whose blood has not been thrown when the altar is flawed?
Critical phase without complete altar - Since at a critical phase there was not a complete altar for throwing, the slaughtered ones become invalid; however, living animals are never rejected.
