🎓 Quiz

הלכות פסולי המוקדשין פרק ט״ז · 5 Questions
Question 1
If a Cohen slaughtered with a thought outside its time, and then received, carried, and sprinkled with a thought outside its place. What is the ruling?
צירוף מחשבת מקום עם מחשבת זמן מונע חלות Pigul, ולכן הקרבן Disqualified only.
Question 2
What creates pigul according to the fundamental principle in the chapter?
The chapter emphasizes that pigul depends on a thought of time free from any other disqualifying combination.
Question 3
Thought of half an olive outside its place and a whole olive outside its time. What is the result?
When there is a complete olive of thought of time, a half-measure of thought of place does not prevent the establishment of pigul.
Question 4
In Minchah: There was a thought of time in the handful but not in the incense. What is the ruling?
In Minchah, a thought of time is needed regarding all permissibles, handful and incense; thinking of only one of them does not create pigul.
Question 5
Leftovers of Minchah were missing before the offering of the handful, and the offering was done with a thought of time. How does Rambam rule?
Rambam is uncertain if the Minchah was established for the path of pigul when the leftovers were missing before the offering.
