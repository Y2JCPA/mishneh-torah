🎓 Quiz

הלכות פסולי המוקדשין פרק א · 5 Questions
Question 1
Who is prohibited from slaughtering an offering initially in this chapter?
Rambam rules that a non-priest can slaughter, but an impure person is prohibited initially due to a decree lest they touch the holy meat.
Question 2
What is the status of sacred offerings slaughtered in the south of the courtyard?
Slaughtering sacred offerings requires the north; slaughter in the south invalidates the offering.
Question 3
Blood that spilled from the animal's neck to the floor, was collected in a serving vessel, and only then accepted—what is the ruling?
Reception must be direct from the animal's neck; collecting from the floor is not considered valid reception.
Question 4
If an invalid person received the blood, but soul blood remains in the neck—what can generally be done?
The chapter teaches that generally there is a remedy for renewed reception from the remaining soul blood, except in the case of an impure person who renders the remainder as a rejection.
Question 5
What is the ruling when the sun has set and the blood of the offering has not yet been injected?
At the end of the chapter, Rambam states that sunset before injection invalidates, and a late injection is not accepted favorably.
