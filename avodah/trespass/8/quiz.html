🎓 Quiz

הלכות מעילה פרק ח · 5 Questions
Question 1
What is the halachic solution for workers in a dedicated orchard?
Direct eating from the sacred is misuse; therefore, provide workers with money to buy regular food.
Question 2
Why are artisans not paid directly with sacred animals or garments?
Maimonides states that sanctity does not transfer to wages except through money, and only then does the object become mundane and can be given to the artisan.
Question 3
How is the Temple built to prevent misuse during construction?
This is a preventive decree: that they should not lean on or benefit from the sacred while building.
Question 4
What is the court's condition regarding priests' use of sacred salt and wood?
The allowance is focused on the needs for eating the sacrifice given to the priests, not for regular private use.
Question 5
What principal insight is emphasized in the last halacha of the chapter?
Maimonides teaches that maintaining laws and judgments together includes reverence even for commandments whose reasons are not clear.
