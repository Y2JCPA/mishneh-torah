🎓 Quiz

הלכות מעילה פרק ב · 5 Questions
Question 1
For kodashim kalim, when does me'ilah begin on the fats and organs?
Halachah 1 states that even if fats were already on the altar, me'ilah on them starts only after the blood is cast.
Question 2
What is the law of the ashes of the red heifer after reduction to ash?
Halachah 5 teaches that despite the parah adumah's sanctity, Beit Din stipulated no me'ilah in its ashes.
Question 3
After a bird sin-offering's blood is presented, which statement is correct?
Halachah 6 distinguishes between ongoing prohibition of benefit and absence of me'ilah liability after the blood rite.
Question 4
Which ash retains me'ilah according to this chapter?
Halachot 14-15: outer-altar ash has me'ilah before and after removal; inner-altar and Menorah ashes are forbidden but without me'ilah.
Question 5
How does me'ilah apply to blemished kodshei kodashim animals before redemption?
Halachah 16 rules that me'ilah remains until redemption regardless of whether blemish preceded consecration or resulted from timing disqualification.
