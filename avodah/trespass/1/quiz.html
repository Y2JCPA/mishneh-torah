הלכות מעילה פרק א · 5 Questions
Question 1
What minimum amount of benefit triggers classic me'ilah liability?
The chapter sets a perutah-value threshold for liability.
Question 2
After a sacrifice becomes permitted for eligible eaters, misuse by an ineligible person is generally classified how?
Because there is a permitted stage for some people, this chapter excludes it from me'ilah classification.
Question 3
Which component is stated as non-essential for atonement completion in inadvertent me'ilah?
The text emphasizes principal plus asham as essential, while the added fifth is not indispensable.
Question 4
If someone brings the guilt-offering before repaying the principal, what is the result?
The sequence matters: bringing the offering first does not satisfy the obligation.
Question 5
How are shearing and labor on animals consecrated for Temple maintenance treated in this chapter?
These acts are prohibited by rabbinic decree here, so Torah lashes do not apply.
