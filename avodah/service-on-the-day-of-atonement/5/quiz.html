הלכות עבודת יום הכיפורים פרק ה · 5 Questions
Question 1
What is the law if the High Priest scoops incense before the slaughter of the bull?
Although the scooping is done in the courtyard, it is a necessary step for the inner service, thus its precedence invalidates.
Question 2
What to do with the blood of the goat in the Holy of Holies before the blood of the bull?
The order is critical: the blood of the bull comes first. Therefore, rectify by completing the correct order and bringing a new goat.
Question 3
What is the process if one of the two goats for Yom Kippur dies after the lottery?
After the lottery, a new pair and a renewed lottery are needed; the excess will graze until it has a blemish and its money will go to charity.
Question 4
What is the ruling if the person designated to send the goat becomes impure?
The verse in the hand of a designated person teaches that the mission remains with the appointee even if he becomes impure.
Question 5
What is the law regarding incense that is missing one ingredient or produces smoke?
Maimonides rules that a deficiency in the incense for the inner sanctum is severe enough to incur the death penalty.
