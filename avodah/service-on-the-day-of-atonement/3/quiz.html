הלכות עבודת יום הכיפורים פרק ג · 5 Questions
Question 1
What prevents the service of the two goats?
The Rambam distinguishes that the lottery itself is a hindrance, even though it is not a classic sacrificial service.
Question 2
How many sprinklings does the High Priest perform?
The chapter explicitly states that the total number of sprinklings is 43, including before the cover, on the curtain, and on the golden altar.
Question 3
Why does the High Priest count aloud?
Thus he clearly distinguishes between the first sprinkling and the seven additional sprinklings, preventing confusion in the count.
Question 4
Why does the High Priest read 'And on the tenth' by heart?
The Rambam gives two reasons: not to roll publicly from place to place, and not to raise suspicion if another Torah is read.
Question 5
Who is fit to lead the goat to Azazel?
In principle, any person is fit for this, but in practice, the High Priests instituted that only a fixed person should lead the Israelite.
