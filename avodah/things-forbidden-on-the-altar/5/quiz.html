הלכות איסורי מזבח פרק ה · 5 Questions
Question 1
When are lashes required for burning chametz or honey?
The obligation is specifically for burning related to the sacrifice; use as fuel only is exempt.
Question 2
What principle applies to burning leftover sacrifices?
The law forbids burning on the altar parts designated for eating or other use, even if they are holy.
Question 3
What is the ruling on a sacrifice obtained through theft?
The sages invalidated in a famous case to maintain the honor of the altar and prevent desecration of God's name.
Question 4
From which sources are meal offerings and libations forbidden?
This chapter lists these invalidations because the sacrifice must come from something permitted and suitable, not from a transgression.
Question 5
What is the ruling regarding salt for sacrifices?
Salt is a mandatory commandment; its absence leads to lashes, but the sacrifice is generally accepted, except where salt is required in the meal offering.
