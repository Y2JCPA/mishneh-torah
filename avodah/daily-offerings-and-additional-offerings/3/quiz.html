הלכות תמידין ומוספין פרק ג · 5 Questions
Question 1
How much incense is offered each day according to the chapter?
In Halacha 2, a daily weight of 100 dinars is established, half in the morning and half between the evenings.
Question 2
What do you do when less than a kav of coals is scattered from the pan?
In Halacha 5, it is stated that if a kav or less is scattered — sweep into the drain.
Question 3
From which source do you light the western lamp after it has been extinguished?
Halacha 13 specifies the western lamp: its lighting after it has been extinguished is from the fire of the outer altar.
Question 4
Why do we not light the menorah's lamps using a regular external lamp?
In Halacha 14, it is emphasized that no other lamp is used due to a lack of respect for the menorah.
Question 5
If the High Priest offered half a barrel in the morning and then died, and no one else has been appointed, what do we do?
Halacha 22: When the High Priest dies after the morning offering and no one else is appointed, the heirs bring a complete tenth and it is offered in its entirety.
