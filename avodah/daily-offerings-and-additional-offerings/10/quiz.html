הלכות תמידין ומוספין פרק י · 5 Questions
Question 1
Which public offering on Yom Kippur is burned and not eaten?
📜 In addition to the sin offering goat that is eaten in the evening, another goat is brought that is burned, and it is the counterpart of the goat sent to Azazel.
Question 2
What is the number of bulls on the third day of Sukkot in the additional offering?
📜 The bulls decrease each day: 13, 12, 11, and so on until 7 on the seventh day.
Question 3
Why was the water hole thinner than the wine hole in the two cups on the altar?
📜 The different diameters balanced the flow rate, so that the water and wine would finish together at the time of the libation.
Question 4
When Shabbat falls on the intermediate days of Sukkot, what happens to the song 'Kol Musdei Eretz'?
📜 On Shabbat, the Shabbat additional song is recited, and the weekday songs of the intermediate days are postponed in accordance with the order of the days.
Question 5
When is it ideally permissible to mix libation wines from different types of offerings?
📜 The prohibition ideally is meant to prevent confusion in meal offerings; after the meal offerings have been sacrificed or have already been mixed properly, it is permitted to mix the wine ideally.
