🎓 Quiz

הלכות תמידין ומוספין פרק ט · 5 Questions
Question 1
What is the order of offering the additional sacrifices when Rosh Hashanah falls on Shabbat?
We follow frequency: Shabbat is more frequent than Rosh Chodesh, and Rosh Chodesh is more frequent than the festival additional offering.
Question 2
Why does the blood of the sin offering take precedence over the blood of the burnt offering?
The sin offering has the advantage of atonement, therefore its blood precedes the blood of the burnt offering.
Question 3
What is the ruling when there is a conflict between frequent and sacred without explicit resolution?
When the issue remains without a definitive resolution, there is permission to prioritize either side.
Question 4
What is an important exception to the rule that the sin offering precedes the burnt offering?
For festival offerings, we sacrifice according to the order that the Torah established in the verses.
Question 5
If there are peace offerings from yesterday and a sin offering from today for eating, which comes first?
Despite the short time remaining for the peace offerings from yesterday, the sanctity of the sin offering as most sacred gives it precedence.
