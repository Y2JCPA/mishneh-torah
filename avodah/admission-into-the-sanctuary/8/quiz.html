🎓 Quiz

הלכות ביאת מקדש פרק ח · 5 Questions
Question 1
According to H17, what is the total number of blemishes that can disqualify a priest from Temple service?
H17 states that combining the blemishes of this chapter and the previous one, there are 140 disqualifying blemishes in total, enumerated one by one across both chapters.
Question 2
The Torah term 'gibein' (H4) is interpreted by the Rambam as referring to which eyebrow condition?
The Rambam identifies gibein as the condition of having no hair whatsoever on the eyebrows, though Rashi interprets the term as overly long eyebrows; both agree both conditions are blemishes.
Question 3
According to H7, how does one measure whether a nose is disproportionately large or small?
H7 states that if one's nose is larger or smaller than one's pinky finger, it is considered a blemish of disproportionate size.
Question 4
Which of the following hand conditions is explicitly listed as a disqualifying blemish in H11?
H11 lists left-handedness among the six blemishes of the hands; however, an ambidextrous person is acceptable.
Question 5
Which of the following is listed in H16 as a disqualifying blemish beyond physical appearance?
H16 lists four non-physical disqualifying conditions: deafness, intellectual or emotional instability, epilepsy (even with infrequent seizures), and severe depression.
