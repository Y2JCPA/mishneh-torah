הלכות מעשה הקרבנות פרק ז · 5 Questions
Question 1
What to do with the sin offering meat after the fats are burned?
In the sin offering eaten, after blood sprinkling and fats burning, the remaining meat is eaten by male priests in the courtyard.
Question 2
Where are sin offerings burned when they are valid?
When sin offerings are not invalid, their burning is done outside Jerusalem at a place called the ash heap.
Question 3
Who is qualified to burn offerings outside the courtyard?
Maimonides rules that this burning is not a priestly work that delays, thus it is valid for a non-priest and at night.
Question 4
Which action in the bird sin offering is critical?
Blood extraction of the bird sin offering for foundation is a necessary law; without extraction, there is no full fulfillment of the required work.
Question 5
Why do those ascending for upper tasks turn left?
If they go right, they will be delayed until the southwestern corner, and there is a concern for smoke affecting water or wine and even the bird.
