🎓 Quiz

הלכות מעשה הקרבנות פרק י · 5 Questions
Question 1
Who is allowed to eat a sin offering, guilt offering, and leftover meal offerings according to this chapter?
These holy offerings are eaten only by male priests and within the courtyard.
Question 2
What is the time for eating a thank offering?
A thank offering is eaten only day and night, unlike peace offerings which are also eaten the next day.
Question 3
Until when is it permitted to eat sacred offerings that are eaten day and night according to Torah law, and what did the sages establish?
According to Torah law, the time extends until dawn, and the sages limited it to noon as a safeguard.
Question 4
What seasoning is prohibited with sacred meat despite regular spices being allowed?
It is prohibited to season with terumah to avoid invalidating it when the time for eating sacred offerings expires.
Question 5
If a priest was impure at the time of blood sprinkling and became pure before burning the fats — does he receive a portion of the meat?
Eligibility requires purity and fitness from the time of sprinkling until the burning of the fats; impurity at the time of sprinkling disqualifies distribution.
