🎓 Quiz

הלכות מעשה הקרבנות פרק ח · 5 Questions
Question 1
21. Which blood is said to require washing on a garment in this chapter?
46. The Rambam limits the law to the blood of a sin offering from a domesticated animal that is fit for sprinkling, and not to the blood of a bird or blood that has already exited the path of fitness.
Question 2
22. What is the law when the blood of a sin offering splashes from one utensil onto a garment, and from the first garment onto a second garment?
47. The second garment is not obligated because the obligation applies to the original splash of blood that is fit for sprinkling, and not to a secondary splash.
Question 3
23. What action is required for a metal utensil in which a sin offering was cooked?
48. In metal, there is rinsing and washing (rinsing in hot and washing in cold), and in a sacred place.
Question 4
24. When does food that touched the flesh of sacred offerings receive its halachic status as sacred?
49. The chapter emphasizes that the application depends on absorption (giving of taste), not merely on contact, and this is a general principle in sacred items.
Question 5
25. If a garment is splashed with the blood of a sin offering, exits the sanctuary, and becomes impure outside—what is the usual solution (not involving the High Priest's garment)?
50. This way, both the requirement for washing in a sacred place and the laws of impurity are fulfilled when it is impossible to bring a contaminated garment into the sanctuary.
