הלכות מעשה הקרבנות פרק י״ד · 5 Questions
Question 1
If someone says, "I promise to bring a burnt-offering" and the separated animal is later stolen, what is the law?
This is a neder (personal obligation), so loss of the first animal does not discharge duty.
Question 2
Which offering type does Rambam state may NOT be brought in partnership?
Two people may share animal or bird offerings, but a meal-offering is not brought as a partnership offering.
Question 3
A person with no sin liability says: "This is my sin-offering." What is the status?
Chatat and asham cannot be volunteered as vow/pledge offerings; they require actual obligation for sin.
Question 4
When is the negative prohibition of bal te’acher violated for vows/pledges?
Failure at the first festival neglects a positive command, but the lav is violated only after all three regalim pass.
Question 5
From whom does the court seize collateral to enforce bringing offerings, according to this chapter?
Mashkon is taken for olah and shelamim obligations; generally not for chatat/asham, except concern for nazirite chatat delay.
