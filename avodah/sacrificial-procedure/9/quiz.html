הלכות מעשה הקרבנות פרק ט · 5 Questions
Question 1
What is the main difference noted in the chapter regarding the guilt offering of a leper compared to other guilt offerings?
The chapter emphasizes an exception in the laws of receiving the blood only; throwing, eating, and other procedures are similar to other guilt offerings.
Question 2
How many types of peace offerings are mentioned in Chapter 9?
Four were counted: communal peace offerings and three types of individual peace offerings (regular, thanksgiving, nazarite).
Question 3
How is the wave offering performed according to the chapter's description?
The wave offering is defined as movement in four directions: bringing and elevating as well as lowering and bringing.
Question 4
Who eats the breast and thigh after the wave offering and burning of the sacrificial parts?
The breast and thigh are priestly gifts and are eaten by the priests after the altar stage.
Question 5
What is the relationship between the bread of the nazarite and the bread of thanksgiving according to the chapter?
The nazarite brings 20 unleavened loaves from a flour amount of 6 and 2/3 tenths, equivalent to two-thirds of the unleavened arrangement of thanksgiving.
