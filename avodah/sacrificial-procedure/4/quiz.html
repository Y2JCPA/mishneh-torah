🎓 Quiz

הלכות מעשה הקרבנות פרק ד · 5 Questions
Question 1
Which acts does this chapter treat as strictly daytime for korban validity?
Halachah 1 states sacrifices are slaughtered by day and blood is sprinkled on the day of slaughter; sunset disqualifies the blood.
Question 2
Why did Chazal set midnight as a limit for burning eimurim and limbs, even though Torah law allows the whole night?
Halachah 2 explains midnight is a protective decree to prevent inadvertent transgression through delay.
Question 3
What is the status of independently donated libations (nesachim) according to this chapter?
Halachah 5 distinguishes independent libations from those accompanying a sacrifice and permits nighttime sanctification and offering.
Question 4
If a basin’s bottom opened before incoming blood reached a resting place in it, what is the ruling?
Halachah 9 rules that blood entering a space where it will not come to rest is not considered received in a sanctifying way.
Question 5
In which four stages must proper intent be present regarding korban type and owner?
Halachah 10 identifies these four as the decisive avodot where intent determines sacrificial standing.
