הלכות מעשה הקרבנות פרק ט״ו · 5 Questions
Question 1
1. When do his words apply?
26. If there was initial intent to exclude the offspring, his words are valid; a later retraction does not apply.
Question 2
2. What is the law regarding the heart?
27. Heart and head are vital organs, thus their consecration applies to the entire animal.
Question 3
3. What to do with the animal?
28. One cannot offer an animal for two contradictory purposes, thus it is redeemed through a blemish and monetary realization.
Question 4
4. What is the law for an impure animal?
29. A species not fit for the altar is not considered a body consecration in this formulation; sometimes only the wording 'for its value' creates a financial obligation.
Question 5
5. What is the ruling for two females?
30. A vow obligates one offering according to the condition; the additional one is sold for that type of offering, but the sale proceeds are not consecrated as an additional obligation.
