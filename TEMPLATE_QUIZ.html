🎓 Quiz

יסודי התורה פרק א׳ · 3 Questions
Question 1
According to the Rambam, what is the "foundation of all foundations"?
The Rambam opens Chapter 1 with: "The foundation of all foundations and the pillar of wisdom is to know that there is a Primary Being who brought into being all existence." This is Halacha 1.
Question 2
What does the Rambam say about physical descriptions of God in the Torah (e.g., "the hand of God")?
In Halacha 9 and 12, the Rambam explains that all physical descriptions of God are metaphorical. "The Torah speaks in the language of man" — these are approximations for concepts beyond human comprehension.
Question 3
How does the Rambam describe God's "oneness"?
In Halacha 7, the Rambam emphasizes that God's unity "surpasses any unity found in the world." He is not one as a species, or as a body with parts — His oneness is absolutely unique and incomparable.
