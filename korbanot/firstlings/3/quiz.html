🎓 Quiz

הלכות בכורות פרק ג · 5 Questions
Question 1
Who can authorize the slaughter of a blemished firstborn animal if no expert is present?
According to H2, if an expert is unavailable, three synagogue members can authorize slaughtering due to obvious blemishes.
Question 2
What must happen if a firstborn animal is slaughtered without expert authorization?
H4 states that slaughtering without expert permission results in the meat being forbidden and requires burial.
Question 3
How much must a non-expert compensates for improperly slaughtering a large firstborn animal?
H6 explains that a non-expert must pay half the worth of a large animal for improper slaughtering.
Question 4
What is the outcome when wool from a firstborn is mixed with other wool?
According to H13, when firstborn shearings mix with others, they all become consecrated due to significant status.
Question 5
Can an expert take a fee for inspecting a firstborn more than once?
H7 specifies that an expert may not take a fee more than once unless allotted by the Sages for continual inspection.
