🎓 Quiz

הלכות בכורות פרק ב · 5 Questions
Question 1
What happens to a firstborn with a permanent blemish?
A permanent blemish allows a firstborn to be slaughtered anywhere, as stated in halacha 1.
Question 2
Is a Cesarean-born animal considered a firstborn?
Halacha 4 explicitly states that Cesarean-born animals are not considered firstborns.
Question 3
What are the consequences if someone intentionally causes a blemish?
Halacha 7 highlights a penalty that delays slaughter until another blemish occurs naturally.
Question 4
Can a priest testify to his own benefit about a blemished firstborn?
The text in halacha 17 allows a priest to testify with corroboration from another not directly benefiting.
Question 5
What is allowed if blood ceases to flow freely in a firstborn?
Halacha 13 permits bloodletting only if it does not intentionally cause a blemish.
