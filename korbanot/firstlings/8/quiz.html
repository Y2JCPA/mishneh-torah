לרפואת פייגא בת יטא רבקה

🎓 Quiz

הלכות בכורות פרק ח · 5 Questions
Question 1
What happens if the owner mistakenly calls the eleventh the tenth but does not call the tenth anything at all?
According to H3, since the name 'tenth' was not removed from the true tenth animal, the eleventh is not consecrated.
Question 2
How are the ninth and tenth treated if they depart together and are called 'the ninth'?
In H4, both the ninth and the tenth are considered consecrated but should only be eaten after contracting a blemish.
Question 3
If counting is interrupted due to external distractions, what should be done?
H13 specifies that interruptions like colleague conversations or nightfall allow resuming the count later.
Question 4
What is the rule when the first two animals depart together?
H6 states that two animals departing as one can be counted as such, affecting which becomes designated as the ninth and tenth.
Question 5
In the case of a tithed animal jumping back into a corral, what happens to the rest?
According to H14, there's doubt if an animal is already tithed or not, thus exempting those in the corral from more tithing.
