🎓 Quiz

הלכות בכורות פרק ד · 5 Questions
Question 1
What happens when a Jew and a gentile co-own a cow, and the gentile owns even a minor part?
Halacha 1 specifies that any gentile ownership interest renders the animal exempt.
Question 2
How do the laws apply if a Jew buys a suckling animal from a gentile?
According to Halacha 8, it's assumed the animal has given birth before, thus exempting the offspring.
Question 3
What is the status of a placenta discharged by a large animal according to the text?
Halacha 10 explains that a placenta discharge indicates a fetus was borne, exempting future births.
Question 4
If an emerging fetus is partially removed by Caesarean and partially from the womb, what is its status?
Halacha 15 clarifies that partial emergence does not meet consecration requirements.
Question 5
How is a firstborn determined if a womb is artificially partitioned between two animals and a fetus traverses them?
According to Halacha 18, the doubt remains if either womb being 'opened' alters firstborn rules.
