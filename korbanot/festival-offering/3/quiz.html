הלכות חגיגה פרק ג · 5 Questions
Question 1
Who is exempt from the mitzvah of Hakhel?
Halacha 2 clearly states that those who are ritually impure are exempt from the mitzvah of Hakhel.
Question 2
Where does the Hakhel reading take place?
Halacha 3 specifies that the Hakhel reading occurs in the Women's Courtyard.
Question 3
What special actions are taken in Jerusalem before the Hakhel reading?
Halacha 4 describes that trumpets are sounded throughout Jerusalem to gather people before the reading.
Question 4
In what language must the Hakhel reading be conducted?
Halacha 5 decrees that the reading must be in Hebrew, referred to as the Holy Tongue.
Question 5
What happens if Hakhel falls on the Sabbath?
Halacha 7 explains that Hakhel is delayed until after the Sabbath if it coincides with it.
