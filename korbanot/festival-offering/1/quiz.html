🎓 Quiz

הלכות חגיגה פרק א · 5 Questions
Question 1
What are the three commandments related to festivals?
As detailed in H1, the commandments are to appear, bring offerings, and rejoice.
Question 2
According to Rabbinic Law, what is the minimum value for a burnt-offering when appearing?
H2 states the burnt-offering should not be worth less than a silver me'ah.
Question 3
Can offerings override Sabbath prohibitions?
H8 explains that festive offerings do not override Sabbath but can proceed on holidays.
Question 4
What is the consequence of not bringing festival offerings if the festival has passed?
H6 explicitly states it's a wrong that cannot be corrected post-festival.
Question 5
What should a person with ample means and limited household members bring more of during a festival?
H11 advises more burnt-offerings for those with ample means and smaller households.
