הלכות חגיגה פרק ב · 5 Questions
Question 1
Who is exempt from the obligation to appear before God on festivals?
Halacha 1 exempts women and those with specific incapacities from festival appearances.
Question 2
What is required for children in terms of appearing before God?
Halacha 3 specifies the obligation to train a child to appear if they can hold a parent's hand.
Question 3
Are individuals who work in loathsome professions required to appear for the festival?
Halacha 2 states that such individuals should purify themselves and appear.
Question 4
Can festive peace-offerings be purchased with funds from the second tithe?
Halacha 8 allows using second tithe funds if intermingled with ordinary ones.
Question 5
What is emphasized about eating festive offerings?
Halacha 14 emphasizes including others such as the Levite, stranger, orphan, and widow.
