🎓 Quiz

הלכות תמורה פרק א · 5 Questions
Question 1
What happens if a Jewish community attempts to transfer the holiness of a sacrificial animal?
H1 states that such transfers by the community are ineffective, although forbidden.
Question 2
Is the transfer of holiness effective when mistakenly transposed from a burnt-offering to a peace-offering?
H2 clarifies that even mistaken transfers are effective, incurring lashes.
Question 3
Can an heir transfer the holiness of a sacrificial animal inherited from their parent?
H5 indicates that partners cannot transfer holiness, but an individual heir can.
Question 4
Why can't the holiness of Temple upkeep animals be transferred?
H12 highlights that these are excluded by association to tithe laws and individual sacrifice rules.
Question 5
Can the holiness be transferred to a hybrid animal?
H17 states holiness cannot be transferred to non-sacrificial-fit animals like hybrids.
