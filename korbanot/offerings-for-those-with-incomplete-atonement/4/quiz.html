🎓 Quiz

הלכות מחוסרי כפרה פרק ד · 5 Questions
Question 1
Why must the person afflicted by tzara'at immerse themselves again on the eighth day?
H1 explains that he immerses on the eighth day due to potential impurity contracted during his affliction.
Question 2
What actions are performed with the guilt-offering and oil during the purification?
H2 specifies tenufah is performed with both the sheep and the oil together, or individually if necessary.
Question 3
Where is the remaining oil applied after the blood is applied during the ritual?
H2 states the oil is applied to the same locations as the blood on the right ear, thumb, and toe.
Question 4
What is the consequence of partaking the log of oil before the relevant rituals are completed?
H3 declares lashes for consumption before completing the sprinklings and blood application.
Question 5
Who is allowed to eat the remainder of the log of oil?
H3 stipulates that only male priests in the Temple Courtyard can consume the remainder.
