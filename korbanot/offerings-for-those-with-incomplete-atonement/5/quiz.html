הלכות מחוסרי כפרה פרק ה · 5 Questions
Question 1
What happens if the oil spills before completing the seven sprinklings?
Halacha 5 specifies that another log of oil must be brought to restart the sprinkling if it spills before completion.
Question 2
Can oil be applied to the thumb and toe before performing the seven sprinklings?
Halacha 6 states that if applied prematurely, the oil must be reapplied after completing the sprinklings.
Question 3
What is required if a guilt offering was brought without proper intent?
Halacha 2 explains that improper intent requires bringing another offering for purification.
Question 4
If an afflicted person was poor when the guilt offering was slaughtered, what offering must they complete?
Halacha 9 clarifies that offerings depend on status at the time of the guilt offering's slaughter.
Question 5
In cases of mixed sacrifices and an afflicted person's death, how is the situation resolved?
Halacha 10 advises transferring property to achieve eligibility for a poor person's sin-offering.
