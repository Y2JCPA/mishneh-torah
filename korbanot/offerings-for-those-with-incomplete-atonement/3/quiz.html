הלכות מחוסרי כפרה פרק ג · 5 Questions
Question 1
What happens if a zav has a zav discharge on the seventh day after immersing?
A zav discharge on the seventh day negates the entire counting, necessitating a restart (Halacha 1).
Question 2
If a zav has a seminal emission on the fifth day of counting, what must he do?
He only nullifies the day of the seminal emission and resumes counting from the following day (Halacha 2).
Question 3
What is the rule for a zav who finds himself pure after not checking throughout the seven days?
He can rely on the presumption of purity if he finds himself pure at the end (Halacha 3).
Question 4
Who can contract zav impurity?
All males, including infants and converts, can contract zav impurity (Halacha 6).
Question 5
In the case of a tumtum with zav discharges, what is required?
A tumtum must bring a sacrifice for zav discharges, but it is not eaten due to doubtful impurity (Halacha 7).
