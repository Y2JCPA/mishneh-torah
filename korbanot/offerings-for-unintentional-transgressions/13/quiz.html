הלכות שגגות פרק י״ג · 5 Questions
Question 1
What happens if a court includes an unfit judge?
According to Halacha 1, if the court is not fully qualified, the transgressors must bring a sin-offering, while the court is exempt.
Question 2
When do two types of transgressions combine?
Halacha 4 states that transgressions can combine to form a majority only if from the same court.
Question 3
How are the inhabitants of the Diaspora viewed?
Halacha 2 specifies that only the inhabitants of Eretz Yisrael are counted, excluding the Diaspora.
Question 4
What occurs if the court acted intentionally?
Halacha 6 clarifies that an intentional court error results in the court being exempt because their ruling was intentional.
Question 5
What is required for a court to be liable?
Halacha 2 explains that the court is liable if a majority of Eretz Yisrael or tribes act erroneously on their ruling.
