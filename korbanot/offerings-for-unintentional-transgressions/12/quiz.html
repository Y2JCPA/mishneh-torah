🎓 Quiz

הלכות שגגות פרק י״ב · 5 Questions
Question 1
What sacrifice is required if the High Court errs concerning false deity worship?
According to Halacha 1, a bull as a burnt-offering and a goat as a sin-offering are required for each tribe if the error concerns false deity worship.
Question 2
When do individuals need not bring a sin-offering for their transgression?
Halacha 1 clarifies that individuals are exempt when they act based on the High Court's erroneous ruling.
Question 3
What must happen for the High Court to not bring a provisional guilt-offering?
Halacha 2 states they are exempt from a provisional guilt-offering if they doubt their ruling since “And the sin becomes known” is not fulfilled.
Question 4
Under what condition would the High Court itself not be liable for an erroneous ruling?
In Halacha 2, one of the conditions for their liability includes the participation of the head of the academy in the ruling.
Question 5
Which condition is necessary for the court's ruling to result in the exemption of individuals from personal offerings?
Halacha 2 requires that the court's erroneous ruling should partially negate a commandment for them to be liable and individuals exempt from offerings.
