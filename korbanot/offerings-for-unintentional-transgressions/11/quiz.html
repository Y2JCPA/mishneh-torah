הלכות שגגות פרק י״א · 5 Questions
Question 1
What conditions must be met to bring an adjustable guilt-offering for impurity?
Halacha 1 clarifies the need for initial and ultimate awareness with a lapse in between for liability.
Question 2
What is the status of bringing a sacrifice if there is unresolved doubt?
Halacha 2 states that one should avoid bringing a sacrifice if there's a doubt, to prevent non-consecrated offerings.
Question 3
If a person immersed after purification rituals then walked another uncertain path, what is the liability?
Halacha 7 indicates definite liability due to uncertainty on both path occasions.
Question 4
What determines offering obligations when a person denies witness claims regarding impurity and Temple entry?
Halacha 8 states witness testimony is accepted over personal denial, obligating a sacrifice.
Question 5
What sacrifice provides atonement if one entered the Temple purposefully while impure?
Halacha 9 specifies the bull offered by the High Priest for priests or a goat for Israelites in cases of intentional impurity.
