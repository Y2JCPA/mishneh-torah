הלכות שגגות פרק א · 5 Questions
Question 1
What does one bring for inadvertently violating a commandment punishable by karet?
A sin-offering is required for such inadvertent violations, as stated in H1.
Question 2
Which transgressions are exceptions to bringing a sin-offering despite being liable for karet?
Blasphemy and some positive commandment negations do not require a sin-offering, as explained in H2.
Question 3
What type of offering does an impure person bring for entering the Temple inadvertently?
An impure person brings an adjustable guilt-offering, highlighted in H3.
Question 4
How are different roles addressed in the bringing of sin-offerings?
Different roles, such as kings and priests, require specific offerings, as detailed in H4.
Question 5
What happens if an entire city inadvertently works on Yom Kippur?
Each individual transgressor must bring their own offerings, as clarified in H5.
