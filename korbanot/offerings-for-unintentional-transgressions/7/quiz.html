🎓 Quiz

הלכות שגגות פרק ז · 5 Questions
Question 1
What determines the number of sin-offerings for idol worship in one lapse of awareness?
According to H1, liability is for each act of service performed in one lapse of awareness.
Question 2
If a person forgets the fundamental principle of the Sabbath, how many sin-offerings are required?
H2 explains that one sin-offering suffices for all transgressions in a single lapse of awareness.
Question 3
How does knowledge of forbidden labors affect liability during the Sabbath?
According to H3, knowing forbidden labors requires an offering for each category of forbidden labor.
Question 4
What happens if a person intends to cut an already severed object on Sabbath but mistakenly cuts one connected?
H11 clarifies that there is no sin-offering as the intended purpose was not fulfilled.
Question 5
When stirring coals on Sabbath, what determines liability for a sin-offering?
H12 states liability is based on intention to perform a forbidden act (extinguishing/igniting).
