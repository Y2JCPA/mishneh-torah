🎓 Quiz

הלכות שגגות פרק ג · 5 Questions
Question 1
What happens if a person remains silent when witnesses claim he committed a transgression?
H2 states that if the person remains silent, they are liable to bring a sin-offering.
Question 2
Can a man bring a sin-offering designated for forbidden fat for a different sin?
According to H3, offerings must be specific to the transgression, otherwise, they are disqualified.
Question 3
If two people bring one animal as a sin-offering for two transgressions, what is to be done?
H4 specifies the animal should pasture until it has a blemish and then be sold.
Question 4
How does Yom Kippur affect the obligation to bring a tentative guilt-offering?
H10 clarifies that belief in Yom Kippur's atonement is necessary for it to count.
Question 5
What happens if someone is about to be executed but their sin-offering animal is ready?
H12 states that execution is postponed for the offering only if the animal has been slaughtered.
