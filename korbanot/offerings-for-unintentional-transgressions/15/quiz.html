🎓 Quiz

הלכות שגגות פרק ט״ו · 5 Questions
Question 1
What animal must an anointed priest bring for an inadvertent transgression?
According to halacha 1, an anointed priest brings a bull for his inadvertent transgressions.
Question 2
Under what condition is a priest exempt from bringing a sacrifice for an inadvertent sin?
Halacha 4 states an anointed priest is exempt if he acts based on a court's mistaken ruling instead of his own.
Question 3
What must happen for a nasi to bring a provisional guilt-offering?
As halacha 6 explains, a nasi brings this offering when in doubt about a transgression.
Question 4
How does the office status of a nasi affect the sacrifice required after a transgression?
Halacha 9 shows the nasi's offer depends on his status during the time of transgression.
Question 5
What happens if an anointed priest transgresses unknowingly before being appointed?
Halacha 10 clarifies he brings an offering like an ordinary person, regardless of post-appointment awareness.
