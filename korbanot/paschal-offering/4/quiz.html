🎓 Quiz

הלכות קרבן פסח פרק ד · 5 Questions
Question 1
What should happen if a Paschal sacrifice was slaughtered without the proper intent?
According to Halacha 1, if a Paschal sacrifice is slaughtered without the correct intent, it is unacceptable and requires a second offering.
Question 2
How should the meat of a Paschal sacrifice be treated if it becomes impure before the blood is poured?
Halacha 2 states the blood should not be poured on the altar if the meat becomes impure before the blood is sprinkled.
Question 3
What happens to a Paschal sacrifice if its owners die before the blood is poured?
Halacha 3 explains that if the owners die before the blood is poured, the sacrifice should wait overnight before being burned.
Question 4
What action should be taken if a Paschal sacrifice is intermingled with a different type of offering?
Halacha 8 states that intermingled sacrifices with other offerings should pasture until blemished.
Question 5
How should leftover funds from a Paschal sacrifice be used?
As explained in Halacha 10, leftover funds are used for a peace-offering.
