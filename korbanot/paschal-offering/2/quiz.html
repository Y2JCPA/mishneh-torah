🎓 Quiz

הלכות קרבן פסח פרק ב · 5 Questions
Question 1
What happens if a Paschal sacrifice is slaughtered for both those who can and cannot consume an olive-sized portion?
H5 explains that if it includes people who can eat an olive-sized portion, it remains valid.
Question 2
What is the consequence if a Paschal sacrifice is slaughtered intending it for uncircumcised individuals?
H6 describes that it becomes invalid if intended for the uncircumcised during key sacrificial acts.
Question 3
Can a person remove themselves from enumeration after a Paschal sacrifice is slaughtered?
H14 states that removal is possible only until slaughter, not after.
Question 4
Which individuals require their explicit consent to be counted for the Paschal offering?
H8 explains that those above majority and wives require consent to be effectively counted.
Question 5
How does the division work if two guardians slaughter Paschal sacrifices for an orphan below majority?
H11 states that an orphan below majority may choose between sacrifices offered by two guardians.
