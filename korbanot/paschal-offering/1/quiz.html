🎓 Quiz

הלכות קרבן פסח פרק א · 5 Questions
Question 1
What happens to someone who willfully neglects offering the Paschal sacrifice?
As stated in H2, willful neglect results in karet.
Question 2
Where must the Paschal sacrifice be slaughtered?
H3 specifies the Temple Courtyard as the place for slaughtering.
Question 3
What is the rule if the Paschal sacrifice is slaughtered before the daily offering?
H4 states the blood must be stirred if slaughtered before the daily offering.
Question 4
How should the Levites participate during the sacrifice?
H11 mentions the Levites reciting the Hallel during the sacrifice.
Question 5
Can the Paschal sacrifice be carried on the Sabbath?
H19 permits carrying the knife in an abnormal manner if necessary.
