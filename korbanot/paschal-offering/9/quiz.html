🎓 Quiz

הלכות קרבן פסח פרק ט · 5 Questions
Question 1
What happens if the meat of a Paschal sacrifice is removed from its company?
According to halacha 2, when meat is removed, it becomes like treifah and is forbidden to be eaten.
Question 2
What is the rule if two companies share a space but must eat separately?
Halacha 3 states the need for a partition and distinct separation between groups eating in one house.
Question 3
What must one do before feeding matzah and bitter herbs to a resident alien?
Halacha 8 clarifies that it is permitted to feed matzah and bitter herbs, but not the sacrifice, to resident aliens.
Question 4
What happens if a divider falls between two companies eating the Paschal sacrifice?
Halacha 5 dictates they should not partake of the sacrifice until the divider is fixed or removed.
Question 5
What prevents someone from partaking of the Paschal sacrifice?
As stated in halacha 9, having uncircumcised sons or servants disqualifies a person from participating.
