Question 1
What happens if one intentionally fails to offer the second Pesach sacrifice after missing the first due to forces beyond control?
H2 specifies that intentional failure warrants karet.
Question 2
Why might the Paschal sacrifice not be accepted if offered on behalf of someone 'on a distant way'?
H3 details that the sacrifice isn't accepted, obligating the second Pesach.
Question 3
What precedence does immersion take in the context of offering a Paschal sacrifice?
H5 explains immersion for maidservants as equivalent to circumcision for servants.
Question 4
What is the implication for a convert in regard to the second Pesach?
H7 states converts are obligated to offer the second Pesach.
Question 5
Under what condition might someone be exempt from offering the second Paschal sacrifice?
H9 clarifies exemptions due to 'distant way'.
