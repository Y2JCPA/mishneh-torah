🎓 Quiz

הלכות קרבן פסח פרק ז · 5 Questions
Question 1
What happens if the majority of Jewish people are impure due to contact with a corpse during the first Pesach?
H1 states that when the majority are impure, they offer the sacrifice in impurity.
Question 2
If half the people are pure and half are impure, with impure women included, what do the impure men do?
H3 states that impure men offer neither the first or second if considered the lesser portion.
Question 3
When can the Paschal sacrifice be eaten in a state of impurity?
H8 explains that the sacrifice can be eaten in impurity when made in impurity from the outset.
Question 4
What is the rule for a Paschal sacrifice if the sacred utensils became impure?
H9 indicates that it should be eaten even if the utensils are impure, to avoid greater impurity issues.
Question 5
In H7, what happens if impurity is doubtful and private?
H7 allows for offering in impurity if a majority is uncertain about impurity in a private domain.
