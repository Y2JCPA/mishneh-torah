🎓 Quiz

הלכות קרבן פסח פרק ח · 5 Questions
Question 1
What must one eat along with the Paschal sacrifice to fulfill the commandment optimally?
Eating the Paschal sacrifice with matzah and bitter herbs is the optimal way, though not indispensable (H2).
Question 2
If one eats the Paschal sacrifice before nightfall, what is the consequence?
Eating it before nightfall violates a positive commandment (H5).
Question 3
What happens if the Paschal sacrifice is roasted and then cooked?
Cooking after roasting makes it invalid, and one is liable (H8).
Question 4
What should be done if the juices of the Paschal sacrifice drip on flour?
A handful from the affected area should be discarded, showing concern for ritual purity (H13).
Question 5
How is the use of terumah oil on the Paschal sacrifice handled if the company is made of Israelites?
If the meat is still raw, it should be washed and dried, but if roasted, the outer portion should be skinned off (H14).
