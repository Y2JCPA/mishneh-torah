🎓 Quiz

הלכות קרבן פסח פרק ג · 5 Questions
Question 1
What happens if a servant slaughters both a kid and a lamb for his master?
Referenced in H1, if a servant slaughters both and the master had no specification, neither can be partaken.
Question 2
What determines whether a second Paschal sacrifice must be brought if instructions are forgotten?
As per H2, the timing of forgetting relative to the altar determines the need for a second offering.
Question 3
In cases of intermingled sacrifices for two companies, what ensures each group’s offering is valid?
Per H7, representatives from each group join and stipulate intentions, ensuring validity.
Question 4
What is the status of sacrifices if hides intermingled before a disqualifying growth is found?
H9 explains that intermingling with a discovered disqualification requires all to be burnt.
Question 5
If the order of slaughter is unknown when a lost sacrifice is found, what is assumed?
H6 clarifies that when order is unknown, both are burnt but they are exempt from a second obligation.
