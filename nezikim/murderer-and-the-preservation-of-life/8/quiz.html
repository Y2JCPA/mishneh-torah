🎓 Quiz

הלכות רוצח ושמירת הנפש פרק ח · 5 Questions
Question 1
Why did Moses set aside three refuge cities in TransJordan even though they couldn't function without the three in Canaan?
Halacha 3 states Moses' reasoning: 'Since a mitzvah came to my hand, I will fulfill it' — even though the cities wouldn't be operative until all six were designated.
Question 2
What is the minimum width of the road leading to a city of refuge?
Halacha 5 specifies that the road to a city of refuge must be at least 32 cubits wide, ensuring that a fleeing killer is not impeded.
Question 3
A killer enters one of the 48 Levite cities (not one of the 6 designated refuge cities) without intending to seek refuge. Is he protected?
Halacha 10 distinguishes: designated refuge cities protect even unintentional entrants; other Levite cities protect only those who enter knowingly with intent to take refuge.
Question 4
Why are snares and rope traps forbidden in refuge cities?
Halacha 8 states that snares cannot be set in refuge cities so that the foot of the blood redeemer will not be commonly found there — keeping the exile safe.
Question 5
In which future era will three additional refuge cities be added, and where?
Halacha 4 specifies that in the days of the Messianic King, three more cities will be added in the territories of the Keni, Kenizi, and Kadmoni — lands promised to Abraham but not yet conquered.
