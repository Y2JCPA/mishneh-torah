דעות פרק ה׳ · 3 Questions
Question 1
What does the Rambam say about getting drunk?
The Rambam states clearly: whoever becomes drunk is a sinner and a disgrace, and loses his wisdom. A sage drinks only enough wine to help digest his food.
Question 2
According to the Rambam, what is the right order of life priorities?
The Rambam gives practical life advice: first secure an income, then purchase a home, then marry. Getting the order wrong leads to financial stress.
Question 3
How should a Torah sage handle business dealings?
The Rambam demands radical honesty in business: a sage's word is his bond, he's strict with himself and generous with others, pays promptly, and never deceives.
