🎓 Quiz

דעות פרק ה׳ · 5 Questions
Question 1
What does the Rambam say about getting drunk?
Whoever becomes drunk is a sinner and a disgrace, losing their wisdom.
Question 2
What is the right order of life priorities?
Practical advice: secure an income first, then a home, then marry. Wrong order leads to financial stress.
Question 3
How should a Torah sage handle business?
Radical honesty: a sage's word is his bond, strict with himself, generous with others, pays promptly.
Question 4
How should a Torah sage dress?
A sage's clothing should be attractive and clean — bringing honor to Torah. Not flashy, not shabby.
Question 5
Should a person give away all their money to charity?
The Rambam forbids renouncing all possessions to become a burden on others. One should sell movable property for charity, but not land or essential assets.
