דעות פרק ז׳ · 3 Questions
Question 1
Is it permitted to share true negative information about someone?
The Rambam makes clear: rechilut (gossip) is forbidden even when the information is entirely true. The prohibition is about the destructive act of spreading information between people.
Question 2
What do the Sages say about the severity of lashon hara?
The Sages stated that lashon hara is equivalent to idolatry, forbidden relations, and murder combined. The Rambam adds that habitual speakers of lashon hara lose their share in the World to Come.
Question 3
What is the difference between revenge and holding a grudge?
The Rambam defines revenge as "you didn't lend me, so I won't lend you." A grudge is "I'll lend it, but I remember you didn't lend me." Both violate Torah. The ideal: erase the incident from your heart completely.
