דעות פרק ד׳ · 5 Questions
Question 1
How full should you eat?
Stop at three-quarters of full satisfaction. Overeating is "like poison to the body."
Question 2
What does the Rambam call exercise?
"As long as one exercises, exerts himself greatly, does not eat to satiation — he will not become ill."
Question 3
What must a Torah sage's city have?
Essential infrastructure: doctor, bloodletter, bathhouse, laundry, fresh water, synagogue, teacher, scribe, charity fund, and court.
Question 4
How many hours of sleep does the Rambam recommend?
The Rambam says to sleep one-third of the 24-hour day — approximately 8 hours.
Question 5
What promise does the Rambam make to someone who follows his health guidelines?
The Rambam guarantees: follow these rules and "you will not become ill throughout your life, until you grow old and die."
