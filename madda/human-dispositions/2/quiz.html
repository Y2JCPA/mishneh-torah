🎓 Quiz

דעות פרק ב׳ · 5 Questions
Question 1
Which traits does the Rambam say to avoid entirely, not even the middle?
Anger and pride are uniquely dangerous — even the middle path is too much. Move to the far extreme of humility and patience.
Question 2
What does the Rambam say about flattery?
The Rambam prohibits "smooth-tongued and luring" speech. Inner and outer self should match.
Question 3
What is the ideal emotional state?
Neither constant joking (leads to immorality) nor depression (blocks spirituality). Genuine, stable happiness.
Question 4
Why does the Rambam compare bad character traits to illness?
Just as a physically sick person perceives bitter as sweet, a morally "sick" person thinks their bad traits are good and can't self-diagnose.
Question 5
What does the Rambam say about speech?
The Rambam values silence: speak only about matters of wisdom or practical necessity. Excessive speech leads to sin.
