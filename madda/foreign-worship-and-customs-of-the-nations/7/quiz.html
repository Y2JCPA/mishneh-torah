🎓 Quiz

עבודה זרה פרק ז · 5 Questions
Question 1
Is the destruction of idols a positive or negative commandment?
The Rambam classifies idol destruction as a positive commandment. In Israel, one must actively seek them out. Outside Israel, destroy when encountered.
Question 2
Can a Jew's idol ever be nullified (made permissible)?
A gentile can nullify their own or another gentile's idol by damaging it. But a Jew's idol can never be nullified — since a Jew has no authority to "cancel" an idol's status.
Question 3
What happens if you bake bread in an oven heated with asherah wood?
The bread is forbidden. The oven must be cooled down and re-heated with permitted fuel before it can be used again. The prohibition traces through the heat source.
Question 4
Items found on top of an idol's head vs. at its feet — is there a difference?
Items on the idol's head are presumed to be offerings and are forbidden. Items at its feet might have just been casually placed there and are permitted.
Question 5
Why is it permitted to use a bathhouse that has an idol inside it?
The famous Mishnah: Rabban Gamliel bathed in a bathhouse with an Aphrodite statue. His answer: "The bathhouse was not made for the idol; the idol was placed in the bathhouse."
