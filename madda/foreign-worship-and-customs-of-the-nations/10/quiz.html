לרפואת פייגא בת יטא רבקה

🎓 Quiz

עבודה זרה פרק י · 5 Questions
Question 1
Under what conditions can a peace treaty be made with idolaters?
The Rambam requires that peace treaties include renunciation of idolatry. Allowing continued idol worship as part of a peace deal is forbidden.
Question 2
Can a Jewish doctor treat an idolater?
The default is forbidden (don't sustain idolaters). But if refusal would create enmity that endangers Jews, treatment is permitted. Practical safety overrides.
Question 3
Can you sell a house in Israel to an idolater?
The prohibition protects the sanctity of the Land. Selling property to idolaters gives them a foothold that undermines the Land's holiness.
Question 4
Why does the Rambam say to support poor idolaters alongside poor Jews?
The Rambam explicitly states: "for the sake of peace" (mipnei darkei shalom). In exile, peaceful coexistence is both practical and required.
Question 5
Do these laws apply the same way in every era?
The Rambam states: "All the above matters apply only in an era when Israel is in exile... or when the idolaters are more powerful." Context determines application.
