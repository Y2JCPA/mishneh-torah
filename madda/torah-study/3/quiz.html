🎓 Quiz

תלמוד תורה פרק ג׳ · 5 Questions
Question 1
Which "crown" is open to all, regardless of birth?
Priesthood belongs to Aaron's descendants, royalty to David's. But the crown of Torah is available to anyone who earns it.
Question 2
Who outranks whom: a mamzer Torah scholar or an ignorant Kohen Gadol?
The Rambam states explicitly: a mamzer who is a Torah scholar takes precedence over an ignorant Kohen Gadol. Knowledge trumps lineage.
Question 3
What is the Rambam's position on being paid for Torah teaching?
The Rambam takes a strong position: using Torah as a source of income degrades it. Work for a living and study in your remaining time.
Question 4
What is the first question asked in the afterlife?
The Rambam says the first aspect of judgment centers on Torah study. Only afterwards are other actions examined.
Question 5
Why is Torah compared to water?
Water doesn't gather on mountaintops but flows to valleys. Similarly, Torah isn't acquired by the arrogant but by the humble and persistent.
