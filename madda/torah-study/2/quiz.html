לרפואת פייגא בת יטא רבקה

🎓 Quiz

תלמוד תורה פרק ב׳ · 5 Questions
Question 1
What happens to a city that doesn't establish a school for children?
The Rambam says the inhabitants are placed under a ban (cherem) until they establish a school. Education is not optional.
Question 2
What is the maximum number of students per teacher?
Maximum 25 students per teacher. 26-40 requires an assistant. Over 40 must split into two classes.
Question 3
Can a neighbor shut down a Torah class due to noise?
Torah education trumps noise complaints. A neighbor cannot prevent someone from teaching children Torah in their home.
Question 4
Why should unmarried men not teach children?
The concern is about the mothers who come to visit their children. An unmarried man teaching creates potential for impropriety.
Question 5
When can a child be transferred to a different teacher?
A child may be transferred to a more capable teacher. Quality of instruction takes priority over teacher loyalty.
