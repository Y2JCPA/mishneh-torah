יסודי התורה פרק ב׳ · 3 Questions
Question 1
According to the Rambam, what leads a person to love God?
In Halacha 2, the Rambam says that when a person contemplates God's "great and wondrous works and creations" and sees the infinite wisdom in them, he immediately loves, praises, and glorifies God.
Question 2
How many ranks of angels does the Rambam describe?
The Rambam lists 10 ranks of angels: Chayot HaKodesh (highest), Ofanim, Erelim, Chashmalim, Seraphim, Malachim, Elohim, Bnei Elohim, Cheruvim, and Ishim (closest to humans).
Question 3
What emotion does a person feel when they realize how small they are compared to God's creation?
The Rambam says that when a person considers the vastness of creation and realizes how "small, lowly, and insignificant" they are, they are immediately filled with awe and fear (yirah) of God.
