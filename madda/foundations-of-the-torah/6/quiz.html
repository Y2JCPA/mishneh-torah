יסודי התורה פרק ו׳ · 5 Questions
Question 1
How many of God's names have full sanctity?
Seven names: YHVH, Kel, Elokim, Kel-okim, Ekyeh, Shakkai, and Tzevakot.
Question 2
What should be done with a vessel bearing God's name?
Cut off the portion bearing the name and bury it properly, even if engraved on metal.
Question 3
Are descriptive titles like "The Merciful" treated like the 7 names?
Titles are descriptions of attributes, not names. They may be erased. Only the 7 specific names have irreversible sanctity.
Question 4
What is the rule about prefix letters before God's name?
Letters added before God's name (like ל, ב, ה) may be erased. But suffixed letters (like the ך in אלהיך) are part of the name and may not be erased.
Question 5
Is every mention of "God" in Tanach sacred?
Context determines sanctity. "God" in the Avraham-angels story is sacred. "God" referring to Michah's idol is not sacred.
