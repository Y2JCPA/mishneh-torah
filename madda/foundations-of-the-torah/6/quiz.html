🎓 Quiz

יסודי התורה פרק ו׳ · 3 Questions
Question 1
How many of God's names have full sanctity and may never be erased?
The Rambam lists exactly 7 names: YHVH (the Tetragrammaton), Kel, Elokim, Kel-okim, Ekyeh, Shakkai, and Tzevakot.
Question 2
What should be done with a vessel that has God's name written on it?
The Rambam rules that when God's name appears on a vessel, one should cut off the portion bearing the name and bury it properly. Even if the name is engraved on metal, it must be handled this way.
Question 3
Are descriptive titles like "The Merciful" or "The Great" treated the same as the 7 names?
The Rambam clearly distinguishes: titles like "Gracious," "Merciful," "Great," "Mighty," "Awesome," and "Faithful" are descriptions of God's attributes, not names. They do not have the same sanctity and may be erased.
